# Community-Led Growth (CLG)

## Problem

Rising CAC, ad fatigue, algorithm changes. Need sustainable, low-cost acquisition channel.

## Framework

CLG = Community of consumers drives growth instead of company marketing.

**Benefits:**
- 24% higher purchase value
- 46% higher CLV
- 32% CAC reduction
- $6.40 ROI per $1 invested

## Platform Selection

| Platform | Best For | Cost | Engagement |
|----------|----------|------|------------|
| Discord | Real-time, multimedia | Free | 94 min/day |
| Slack | B2B, professional | $12.50/user | Structured |
| Circle | Course creators | Paid | Owned |
| Reddit | Public discussions | Free | Varies |

**Discord (recommended for 2026):**
- 259M monthly active users
- 73% DAU/MAU ratio
- AI moderation enables 10K+ members with 1 mod
- Non-gaming overtook gaming in growth

## Building Communities

### 1. Start Small
- Quality over quantity
- Foster genuine connections
- 10-50 engaged members better than 1,000 lurkers

### 2. Provide Value
- Exclusive content, early access
- Expert AMA sessions
- Peer networking opportunities
- Member-only perks

### 3. Enable Participation
- User-generated content channels
- Member spotlights
- Challenges and events
- Co-creation opportunities

### 4. Consistent Nurturing
- Active moderation
- Weekly events/discussions
- Recognize top contributors
- Align with business goals

## 2026 Trends

**Micro-Communities:**
- 2.3x higher engagement than large platforms
- More trust, intimate environments

**Tokenized Rewards:**
- 41% plan adoption by 2026
- 25-40% retention improvement

**Value Alignment:**
- Sustainability, inclusivity, transparency = table stakes

## Success Examples

- Notion: Template sharing community
- Figma: Design community, plugins
- Webflow: No-code designer community
- Duolingo: Language learning forums
