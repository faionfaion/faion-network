# Copywriting Fundamentals - Checklist

## Pre-Implementation

- [ ] Identify target audience segments and their pain points
- [ ] Conduct voice of customer research (interviews, reviews, support tickets)
- [ ] Document customer language and exact phrases they use
- [ ] Choose primary copywriting formula (AIDA, PAS, BAB, 4Ps)
- [ ] Write 10 headline variations
- [ ] Research headline formulas and test specificity vs. curiosity
- [ ] Map all product features to customer benefits
- [ ] Write body copy using chosen formula (short sentences, short paragraphs)

## Implementation Phase

- [ ] Add power words (urgency, exclusivity, trust, ease, results)
- [ ] Handle main objections with specific responses
- [ ] Create strong CTAs with action verbs
- [ ] Add social proof and risk reversal elements
- [ ] Optimize for readability (6th-8th grade level, <20 word sentences)
- [ ] Test copy variations with target audience
- [ ] Measure conversion impact of copywriting changes
