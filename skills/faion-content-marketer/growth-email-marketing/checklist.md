# Email Marketing - Checklist

## Pre-Implementation

- [ ] Define email marketing goals and KPIs (open rate, CTR, conversions)
- [ ] Segment email list by customer stage and behavior
- [ ] Create email templates aligned with brand
- [ ] Write compelling subject lines using formulas
- [ ] Plan welcome email series (3-5 automated emails)
- [ ] Create nurture sequence for prospects
- [ ] Design re-engagement campaigns for inactive subscribers
- [ ] Write product/feature announcement emails

## Implementation Phase

- [ ] Create promotional email templates
- [ ] Set up abandoned cart sequences (if e-commerce)
- [ ] A/B test subject lines and send times
- [ ] Monitor email engagement metrics weekly
- [ ] Clean list of non-engaged subscribers quarterly
- [ ] Build email templates library
- [ ] Establish email frequency and schedule
