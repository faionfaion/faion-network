## ai-content-strategy: AI Content Strategy

### Problem

AI-generated content is flooding the market. Standing out requires strategic AI + human collaboration.

### Solution

**Content Stack:**
```
AI Research → AI Draft → Human Expert Edit → QA → Publish
```

**AI Responsibilities:**
- Keyword research, topic brainstorming
- Outline generation, first drafts
- SEO optimization, metadata
- Content repurposing across formats
- Personalization at scale

**Human Responsibilities:**
- Strategy and positioning
- Expert insights and first-hand experience
- Brand voice enforcement
- Fact verification
- Original insights AI hasn't seen

**Google E-E-A-T (Experience):**
- Share first-hand experiences
- Demonstrate real expertise (not aggregated info)
- Show credentials and authority

**Content Differentiation:**

| Generic AI Content | Differentiated Content |
|-------------------|------------------------|
| Summary of top 10 results | Original research/data |
| Generic advice | Specific case studies |
| Surface-level | Expert depth |
| No opinion | Strong POV with reasoning |
| No examples | Real examples with outcomes |
| Product description | How it feels to use |

**Best Practices:**
- Don't "prompt and publish"
- Add proprietary data and insights
- Include expert quotes and interviews
- Share real experiences and failures
- Take positions on controversial topics
- Be specific about target audience
- Keep human oversight
- Set clear boundaries for AI use

**AI Content Tools:**
- Content creation: ChatGPT, Jasper, Copy.ai
- SEO optimization: Surfer, MarketMuse, Clearscope
- Multi-channel: Blaze.ai
- Ad copy: Persado, Phrasee

**Stats:**
- 77% marketing departments adopt AI
- 51% sales teams adopt AI
- Over half of gen AI enterprise budgets → sales/marketing
