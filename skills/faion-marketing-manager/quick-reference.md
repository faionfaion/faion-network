# Marketing Quick Reference

Fast lookup tables for common marketing decisions and benchmarks.

---

## Copy Frameworks

| Framework | When to Use | Structure |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **AIDA** | General conversion copy | Attention → Interest → Desire → Action |
| **PAS** | Aware audience with known pain | Problem → Agitate → Solution |
| **BAB** | Transformation stories | Before → After → Bridge |
| **FAB** | Feature-focused products | Features → Advantages → Benefits |
| **4Ps** | Product positioning | Picture → Promise → Prove → Push |
| **PPPP** | Sales copy | Picture → Promise → Proof → Push |

**Most Common:** AIDA (general), PAS (pain-aware audiences)

## Key Metrics by Funnel Stage

| Funnel Stage | Primary Metric | Secondary Metrics | Target Benchmark |
|--------------|----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Awareness** | Impressions, Reach | Brand searches, social mentions | Industry-dependent |
| **Acquisition** | CAC, CPC, CPM | CTR, conversion rate | CAC < 1/3 LTV |
| **Activation** | Activation Rate | Time to value, feature adoption | 40%+ activation |
| **Retention** | Churn Rate, DAU/MAU | Cohort retention, engagement | <5% monthly churn |
| **Revenue** | LTV, ARPU, MRR | Expansion revenue, upsell rate | LTV:CAC > 3:1 |
| **Referral** | Viral Coefficient (K) | NPS, referral rate | K > 0.5 |

**Key Formula:** LTV:CAC ratio should be > 3:1 for healthy growth.

## Email Campaign Types

| Campaign Type | When to Use | Email Count | Duration | Primary Goal |
|---------------|-------------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| **Welcome Sequence** | New subscribers | 3-5 emails | 7-14 days | Introduce brand, set expectations |
| **Lead Nurture** | Cold/warm leads | 5-10 emails | 2-8 weeks | Move to purchase decision |
| **Re-engagement** | Inactive users (30-90 days) | 2-3 emails | 7-14 days | Win back dormant users |
| **Onboarding** | New customers | 5-7 emails | 2-4 weeks | Drive activation, reduce churn |
| **Newsletter** | Engaged audience | 1 email | Weekly/bi-weekly | Build relationship, share value |
| **Promotional** | Sales/launches | 1-3 emails | 3-7 days | Drive conversions |

## Landing Page Conversion Benchmarks

| Element | Benchmark | Good | Excellent |
|---------|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Bounce Rate** | 40-60% | 30-40% | <30% |
| **Conversion Rate** | 2-5% | 5-10% | >10% |
| **Page Load Time** | <3 seconds | <2 seconds | <1 second |
| **CTA Clicks** | 5-10% | 10-15% | >15% |
| **Form Completion** | 10-20% | 20-30% | >30% |

**Note:** Benchmarks vary by industry and offer type. SaaS tends higher, e-commerce lower.

## SEO Keyword Difficulty

| Difficulty Score | Competition | Strategy | Timeline |
|------------------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **0-30** | Low | Target immediately | 1-3 months |
| **31-50** | Medium | Build topic cluster | 3-6 months |
| **51-70** | High | Long-term content strategy | 6-12 months |
| **71-100** | Very High | Likely not worth it | 12+ months |

**Focus:** Low-hanging fruit (0-30) + strategic medium-difficulty (31-50).

## Paid Ads Budget Allocation

| Channel | % of Budget | Rationale |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| **Search (Google)** | 40-50% | High intent, bottom funnel |
| **Social (Meta/LinkedIn)** | 30-40% | Awareness, retargeting |
| **Display/Video** | 10-20% | Brand awareness, top funnel |
| **Experimental** | 10% | Test new channels |

**Rule:** Start with search (intent), add social (scale), test others (discovery).

## Email Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example | Use Case |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Number + Adjective + Keyword + Promise** | "7 Proven Ways to Double Your Conversion Rate" | List posts, guides |
| **Question** | "Are You Making These 5 Landing Page Mistakes?" | Engagement, curiosity |
| **How to** | "How to Write Copy That Converts in 2026" | Educational, tutorial |
| **Urgency** | "Last Chance: 50% Off Ends Tonight" | Promotions, sales |
| **Personalization** | "{{Name}}, Your Custom Growth Plan Is Ready" | Nurture, onboarding |
| **Curiosity Gap** | "The One Thing We Changed That 3x'd Our Revenue" | Storytelling, case studies |

**Best Practice:** A/B test subject lines. Aim for 40-50 characters (mobile display).
