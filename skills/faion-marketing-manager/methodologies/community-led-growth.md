## community-led-growth: Community-Led Growth (CLG)

### Problem

Ad fatigue, algorithm changes, rising CAC. Need sustainable growth channel.

### Solution: Build Communities That Drive Growth

**Why CLG in 2026:**
- Community customers spend 24% more per purchase
- Active communities = 46% higher CLV
- Word-of-mouth increases conversions by 22%
- CAC reduced by average 32%
- Every $1 invested returns $6.40 in value
- 44% brands see positive ROI within first year
- 58% companies say community members convert faster

**CLG Definition:**
```
Old: Company promotes brand
New: Community of consumers does the legwork
```

**Key Trends 2026:**

1. **Micro-Communities**
   - Shift from large platforms to smaller, specialized spaces
   - Micro-communities = 2.3x higher engagement
   - More trust, intimate environments

2. **Tokenized Rewards**
   - 41% of communities plan to adopt by 2026
   - Gamification improves retention 25-40%

3. **Value Alignment**
   - Sustainability, inclusivity, mental health, transparency
   - Not buzzwords - expectations

4. **Participation Over Transaction**
   - People want to belong, not just buy
   - Treat customers as collaborators

**Platform Comparison:**

| Platform | Best For | Cost | Engagement |
|----------|----------|------|------------|
| Discord | Real-time, multimedia | Free | 94 min/day avg |
| Slack | Professional, text-focused | $12.50/user | Structured threads |
| Circle | Course communities | Paid | Owned experience |
| Reddit | Public discussions | Free | Varies |

**Discord Stats (2026):**
- 656M registered users
- 259M monthly active users
- 94 minutes daily engagement (vs 30-40 Instagram, 20 TikTok)
- 73% DAU/MAU ratio (highest in industry)
- No CPM inflation (rates stable 2+ years)
- Non-gaming communities overtook gaming in growth

**Slack Communities:**
- Online Geniuses: 53,000+ marketers
- Traffic Think Tank: Paid, high-level SEO/growth
- Good for B2B, professional networking

**Discord Revenue Potential:**
- Creators collectively generating $200-500M annually (up from $50M in 2025)
- AI-powered moderation: 10K+ members with 1 mod + AI

**Building Successful Communities:**

1. **Start Small**
   - Quality over quantity initially
   - Foster genuine connections

2. **Provide Value**
   - Exclusive content, early access
   - Expert access, networking

3. **Enable Participation**
   - User-generated content
   - Member-to-member connections

4. **Consistent Nurturing**
   - Ongoing moderation and guidance
   - Keep aligned with business goals

**Success Examples:**
- Notion, Figma, Webflow, Duolingo
- All leveraged CLG for growth

**Social Commerce Context:**
- Projected $2.9 trillion by 2026
- Creators = key source of discovery, trust, loyalty
