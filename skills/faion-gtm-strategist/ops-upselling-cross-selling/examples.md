# Ops Upselling Cross Selling - Examples

### Example 1: SaaS Upsell

**Product:** Email marketing tool

**Trigger:** User hitting 1,000 subscriber limit

**Approach:**
1. In-app notification at 80% capacity
2. Email at 90% capacity
3. Upgrade prompt at 100%

**Message:**
```
Your list is growing! You've reached 900 of 1,000 subscribers.

Upgrade to Growth ($49/mo) for:
- 5,000 subscribers
- Advanced automation
- Priority delivery

[Upgrade Now - First month 50% off]
```

**Result:**
- 40% upgrade within 7 days of limit
- 25% use discount offer
- Expansion MRR: +15%
