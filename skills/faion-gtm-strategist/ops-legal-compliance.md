## Overview

Legal compliance for online businesses covers business structure, policies (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Refund), regulations (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM), intellectual property protection, and contracts.

This methodology is split into focused sub-components:

---

## Sub-Components

### 1. Legal Basics
**File:** [ops-legal-basics.md](ops-legal-basics.md)

**Coverage:**
- Business structure (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp)
- Essential policies (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Refund)
- Key regulations (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, COPPA)
- Intellectual property (Trademarks, Copyrights, Patents)
- Contract essentials
- Examples (SaaS, Info Product)
- Resources and tools

**Use when:** Setting up legal foundation, choosing business entity, creating policies

---

### 2. Legal Compliance Checklist
**File:** [ops-legal-compliance-checklist.md](ops-legal-compliance-checklist.md)

**Coverage:**
- Legal checklist by stage (Day 1, Month 1, Year 1, Ongoing)
- Website legal requirements
- Policy update process
- Implementation checklist (Business, Policies, Compliance, Protection)
- Common mistakes

**Use when:** Implementing legal compliance, tracking progress, updating policies
