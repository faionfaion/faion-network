# Ops Legal Basics - Checklist

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1Choose Business Structure

**Entity options:**

| Entity | Liability Protection | Taxes | Best For |
|--------|---------------------|-------|----------|
| **Sole Proprietorship** | None | Personal | Testing ideas |
| **LLC** | Yes | Pass-through | Most solopreneurs |
| **S-Corp** | Yes | Salary + distributions | $100K+ income |
| **C-Corp** | Yes | Corporate | Raising investment |

**LLC recommendation for most solopreneurs:**
- Personal liability protection
- Simple taxation (pass-through)
- Credibility with customers
- Flexible structure

**Formation steps:**
1. Choose state (home state or Delaware/Wyoming)
2. File articles of organization
3. Get EIN from IRS
4. Open business bank account
5. Register for state taxes if needed

### Step 2Create Essential Policies

**Required for most online businesses:**

| Policy | What It Covers | Why Required |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| **Terms of Service** | User rules, liabilities | Limits your liability |
| **Privacy Policy** | Data collection and use | Legally required |
| **Cookie Policy** | Cookie usage | GDPR/CCPA compliance |
| **Refund Policy** | Return and refund terms | Customer clarity |

**Terms of Service essentials:**

```markdown
## Key Sections

1. **Acceptance of Terms**
   - Using the service means agreeing

2. **Description of Service**
   - What you provide

3. **User Responsibilities**
   - Acceptable use
   - Account security

4. **Payment Terms**
   - Pricing, billing, cancellation

5. **Intellectual Property**
   - Who owns what

6. **Disclaimers**
   - No warranties
   - Limitation of liability

7. **Termination**
   - How either party can end

8. **Governing Law**
   - Which jurisdiction applies
```

**Privacy Policy essentials:**

```markdown
## Key Sections

1. **Information Collected**
   - What data you collect
   - How you collect it

2. **Use of Information**
   - Why you collect data
   - How you use it

3. **Data Sharing**
   - Third parties you share with
   - Why you share

4. **Data Retention**
   - How long you keep data

5. **User Rights**
   - Access, correction, deletion
   - GDPR/CCPA rights

6. **Security**
   - How you protect data

7. **Contact**
   - How to reach you
```

### Step 3Understand Key Regulations

**Major privacy regulations:**

| Regulation | Applies To | Key Requirements |
|------------|------------|------------------|
| **GDPR** | EU users | Consent, data rights, DPO |
| **CCPA/CPRA** | California users | Disclosure, opt-out, access |
| **CAN-SPAM** | US email marketing | Unsubscribe, accurate headers |
| **COPPA** | Children under 13 | Parental consent |

**GDPR compliance checklist:**
- [ ] Privacy policy explains data use
- [ ] Consent for non-essential cookies
- [ ] Data processing agreement with vendors
- [ ] Respond to access/deletion requests
- [ ] Report breaches within 72 hours

**CAN-SPAM compliance:**
- [ ] Don't use deceptive subject lines
- [ ] Include physical address
- [ ] Working unsubscribe link
- [ ] Honor opt-outs within 10 days

### Step 4Protect Intellectual Property

**IP types:**

| Type | What It Protects | How to Get |
|------|------------------|------------|
| **Trademark** | Names, logos, slogans | Registration (USPTO) |
| **Copyright** | Creative works | Automatic (registration optional) |
| **Patent** | Inventions | Application (expensive) |
| **Trade Secret** | Confidential info | Keep it secret |

**Trademark priorities:**
1. Company name
2. Product name
3. Logo
4. Tagline (if distinctive)

**Before launch:**
- Search USPTO for conflicts
- Search domain availability
- Check social media handles
- Consider trademark registration

### Step 5Contract Essentials

**Contracts you may need:**

| Contract | When to Use | Key Terms |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Customer agreement** | Selling services | Scope, payment, liability |
| **Contractor agreement** | Hiring help | IP assignment, confidentiality |
| **NDA** | Sharing confidential info | Definition, duration, exclusions |
| **Affiliate agreement** | Partnership referrals | Commission, terms, compliance |

**Contractor agreement essentials:**
- Work is "work for hire" (you own it)
- IP assignment clause
- Confidentiality clause
- Independent contractor status
- Payment terms
