# Ops Legal Basics - Examples

### Example 1: SaaS Legal Setup

**Business:** Project management SaaS

**Legal foundation:**
- Delaware LLC
- Terms of Service with SaaS-specific clauses
- Privacy Policy (GDPR compliant)
- Cookie consent banner
- DPA for enterprise customers

**Key terms in ToS:**
- Service availability (99.9% target, not guaranteed)
- Data ownership (customer owns their data)
- Limitation of liability (subscription fees)
- Automatic renewal with 30-day notice
