## Business Analysis Framework Modern Application (2025-2026)

### Problem

Business Analysis Framework was published in 2015. Organizations question its relevance to modern contexts (AI, agile, digital transformation). BAs need guidance on applying BA Framework in contemporary environments.

### Solution

Apply Business Analysis Framework with modern interpretations. Leverage the updated Business Analysis Standard for current practices.

| Knowledge Area | Modern Application |
