## Problem

You post randomly on social media with no strategy. You get inconsistent results, waste time creating content nobody sees, and watch competitors build audiences while you stay stuck.

Most social media fails because people spread across too many platforms, focus on follower count instead of engagement, and give up before momentum builds.

### Step 1: Platform Selection

**Choose based on your audience and content type:**

| Platform | Best For | Content Types | Audience |
|----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Twitter/X** | Thought leadership | Text, threads | Tech, creators |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, professional | Articles, carousels | Professionals |
| **Instagram** | Visual brands | Images, reels | Gen Z, millennials |
| **TikTok** | Entertainment, reach | Short video | Gen Z |
| **YouTube** | Long-form education | Video | All ages |
| **Reddit** | Community, authenticity | Text, discussion | Tech, niche |

**Platform fit matrix:**

```markdown

| Criteria | Twitter | LinkedIn | Instagram |
|----------|---------|----------|-----------|

| **Total** | | | |

```

**Rule:** Master ONE platform before adding another.

### Step 2: Content Pillars

**Define 3-5 content themes:**

```markdown
## Content Pillars for [Brand]

1. **[Pillar 1]** (40%) - [Description]
2. **[Pillar 2]** (30%) - [Description]
3. **[Pillar 3]** (20%) - [Description]
4. **Behind the scenes** (10%) - Personal, human

Example for SaaS founder:
1. Product updates (40%)
2. Industry insights (30%)
3. Building in public (20%)
4. Personal wins/fails (10%)
```

### Step 3: Content Formats

**Twitter/X content types:**

| Format | Purpose | Structure |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Tweet** | Insight | 1 idea, <280 chars |
| **Thread** | Education | Hook + 5-10 tweets |
| **Quote tweet** | Engagement | Add value to others |
| **Poll** | Engagement | Opinion questions |

**LinkedIn content types:**

| Format | Purpose | Structure |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Text post** | Thought leadership | Hook, story, lesson |
| **Carousel** | Education | 5-10 slides |
| **Article** | Deep dive | Long-form |
| **Video** | Personality | <3 minutes |

**Instagram content types:**

| Format | Purpose | Structure |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Feed post** | Value | Single image + caption |
| **Carousel** | Education | 5-10 slides |
| **Reels** | Reach | 15-60 seconds |
| **Stories** | Behind scenes | Casual, daily |

### Step 4: Content Calendar

**Posting frequency by platform:**

| Platform | Minimum | Optimal |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Twitter/X | 1/day | 3-5/day |
| LinkedIn | 3/week | 1/day |
| Instagram | 3/week | 1/day |
| TikTok | 1/day | 3/day |
| YouTube | 1/week | 2/week |

**Weekly content template:**

```markdown
## Week of [Date]

### Monday
- [ ] Twitter: Thread on [topic]
- [ ] LinkedIn: Carousel on [topic]

### Tuesday
- [ ] Twitter: 3 tweets
- [ ] Instagram: Reel

### Wednesday
- [ ] Twitter: Quote tweet + 2 tweets
- [ ] LinkedIn: Text post

### Thursday
- [ ] Twitter: Poll + 2 tweets
- [ ] Instagram: Carousel

### Friday
- [ ] Twitter: Thread (recap)
- [ ] LinkedIn: Personal story
```

### Step 5: Engagement Strategy

**The 5-5-5 method (daily):**

1. Spend 5 minutes engaging with accounts you follow
2. Spend 5 minutes replying to comments on your posts
3. Spend 5 minutes finding new accounts to engage with

**Engagement tactics:**

- Reply to comments within 1 hour
- Ask questions at end of posts
- Tag relevant people (sparingly)
- Join trending conversations
- DM new followers (authentic, not salesy)

## Examples

### Example 1: B2B SaaS Founder

**Platform:** Twitter + LinkedIn
**Posting:** Twitter 5/day, LinkedIn 5/week

**Pillars:**
1. Building in public (product updates)
2. SaaS growth tactics
3. Founder lessons
4. Team/culture

**Results after 6 months:**
- Twitter: 0 to 15K followers
- LinkedIn: 500 to 8K followers
- 200 email signups/month from social

### Example 2: Course Creator

**Platform:** Twitter + YouTube
**Posting:** Twitter 3/day, YouTube 1/week

**Pillars:**
1. Industry expertise
2. Student success stories
3. Free tips from course content
4. Personal journey

**Results after 6 months:**
- Twitter: 2K to 25K followers
- YouTube: 0 to 10K subscribers
- Course sales: 50% from social

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Choose 1-2 platforms
- [ ] Define 3-5 content pillars
- [ ] Set posting frequency
- [ ] Create content calendar
- [ ] Batch create 2 weeks of content
- [ ] Schedule posts
- [ ] Set daily engagement time (30 min)
- [ ] Track metrics weekly
- [ ] Analyze what works monthly
- [ ] Adjust strategy quarterly

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| Too many platforms | Spread thin | Master 1 first |
| Inconsistent posting | Algorithm punishes | Batch and schedule |
| All promotion | People unfollow | 80% value, 20% promo |
| No engagement | One-way broadcast | Spend time engaging |
| Chasing followers | Vanity metric | Focus on engagement |
| Giving up too soon | Momentum takes time | Commit to 6 months |

## Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Shows | Goal |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Followers | Reach potential | Growing |
| Engagement rate | Content quality | >3% |
| Link clicks | Interest | Growing |
| Profile visits | Curiosity | Growing |
| Conversions | Business impact | Growing |

**Engagement rate formula:**
```
Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions x 100%
```
