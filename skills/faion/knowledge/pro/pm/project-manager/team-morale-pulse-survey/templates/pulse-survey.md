# Weekly Team Pulse — Sprint [N], Week ISO [YYYY-Www]

This survey is anonymous. The PM acts on alerts within 48 business hours. No retaliation.

1. On a scale of 0–10, how likely are you to recommend this team as a place to work? (eNPS)
2. On a scale of 1–10, how is your workload this week? (1 = drowning, 10 = ideal)
3. On a scale of 1–10, how much autonomy do you have over what you work on?
4. On a scale of 1–10, how clear are the goals for this sprint?

Optional: anything you want the PM to know? (PM-only; never quoted verbatim in team channels)
