# Escalation Conversation Script

## Summary

Stakeholder-engagement methodology produces RACIs and influence maps. What it doesn't give the PM is the first 30 seconds of the actual phone call when a client says "we have a serious concern" or a vendor admits the milestone slipped. This methodology defines scripted openers, three de-escalation patterns (acknowledge-park-redirect, fact-fork, costed-options), the decision rule for when to switch from verbal to written, and the follow-up commitment shape that closes the conversation auditable.

## Applies If

- The PM regularly handles cross-organisation conversations under pressure (client unhappy, vendor late, executive escalating, distressed-project rescue).
- The PM has at least one decision lever (re-scope, re-plan, replace vendor, refund, fire customer) to bring to the conversation.
- Conversations recur often enough that improvisation is expensive (≥1/month in this role).
- A written follow-up channel exists where commitments can be logged.

## Skip If

- Internal-only stakeholders where the PM has authority and rapport — informal flow is faster.
- Hostage-negotiation-level conflict requiring legal counsel — escalate to legal/exec, not to a script.
