# PM Certification Exam Changes 2026

## Summary

The PMP exam ECO (Examination Content Outline) changes effective July 1, 2026: People domain drops from 42% to 33%, Process drops from 50% to 41%, and Business Environment rises from 8% to 26%. The +18% shift requires new emphasis on business case development, benefits realization, strategic alignment, governance and compliance, sustainability, and tailoring.

## Why

Exam prep materials built for the 2021 ECO weight Business Environment at 8% — well below the new 26%. Candidates who study old outlines, training courses, or flashcard decks without updating them will be under-prepared for the largest weight area on the new exam. Materials must be rebuilt from the 2026 ECO PDF, not paraphrased from memory.

## When To Use

- Preparing PMP/CAPM candidates for the July 1, 2026 exam revision.
- Updating internal PM training curricula or study-guide repos against the new domain weights.
- Building a study-companion agent that needs a current domain weight map.
- Auditing existing exam prep materials (Rita Mulcahy, PM PrepCast, internal decks) to find content gaps.

## When NOT To Use

- Candidates sitting before the July 1, 2026 cutover — the old outline is still authoritative.
- Non-PMI certifications (PRINCE2, IPMA, AgilePM, Scrum.org) — different bodies, different outlines.
- General PM coaching that is not exam-bound — exam weights distort real-world emphasis.
- Practitioners who already passed — this content is exam-shaped, not job-shaped.
