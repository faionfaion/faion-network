Glassdoor Response Template
===========================

Format: acknowledge → describe action → close positively
Hard limit: 80 words
Tone: professional, specific, no defensiveness

---

POSITIVE REVIEW RESPONSE:
Thank you for sharing this, [first name if given]. [Specific callout from their review —
e.g., "We're glad the L&D budget has been useful."]. [Optional: one sentence on what's
next — e.g., "We're expanding the program in Q3."]. We appreciate you taking the time.

---

MIXED OR NEGATIVE REVIEW RESPONSE:
Thank you for this honest feedback. [Acknowledge the specific concern — e.g., "The
communication gap during the reorg was real and we know it affected many people."].
[Describe action taken or planned — ONLY if true. Never invent.]. We welcome the chance
to do better and encourage direct feedback to [email or HR contact].

---

RULES:
- Never use: "We are committed to...", "Our amazing team...", "We take this seriously..."
- Never dispute employee experience — ever
- Never auto-publish — always human approval before submit
- If the review mentions a specific manager: escalate to HR, do not respond publicly
- If the review mentions compensation/benefit claims that may be inaccurate: legal review first
- Mark UNVERIFIED any specific claim you cannot verify before the response is approved
