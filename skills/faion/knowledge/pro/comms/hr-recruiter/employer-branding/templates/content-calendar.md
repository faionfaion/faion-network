## UTM Tracking Convention

All careers page links: `?utm_source=<channel>&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=employer-brand-<YYYY-MM>`

## Approval Requirements

- Any post with DEI claims: HRBP sign-off required
- Any post with employee name/photo: written consent on file
- Any Glassdoor response: brand manager sign-off before post
- No auto-publish: all posts queue in Buffer/Hootsuite for human final send
