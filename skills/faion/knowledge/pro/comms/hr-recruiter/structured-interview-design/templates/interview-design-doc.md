## Interview Structure

### Round 1: Recruiter Screen (30 min)
**Format:** Phone or video
**Competencies covered:** Background verification, motivation, basic qualifications

**Questions:**
1. "Walk me through your background and what brought you to this opportunity."
2. "What interests you about this role and company specifically?"
3. "Tell me about your experience with [key required skill]."

## Debrief Protocol

1. All interviewers submit scorecard before debrief meeting.
2. Recruiter reads scores aloud; interviewers do not share verbally until all scores are on the table.
3. Discuss evidence per competency, not overall impressions.
4. Document hire decision with cited evidence.
