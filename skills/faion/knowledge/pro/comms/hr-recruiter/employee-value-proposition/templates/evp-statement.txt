EVP Statement Template
======================

Fill in each bracket. Every claim must have a proof point.

For [target candidates — be specific: "ambitious engineers with 3-8 years exp"]
Who want [career goal or motivation — be specific: "to own products end-to-end"]
[Company Name] offers [key differentiating benefits — 2-3 specific, provable claims]
That enable [outcomes for the employee — concrete, not generic]
Unlike [competitor type or alternative path]
We [unique value only you can truthfully claim — must be verifiable]

---

Example (tech startup):

For ambitious engineers who want outsized impact early in their career,
TechCo offers startup ownership, a published IC1-IC6 career ladder, and
a $5K annual L&D budget — unlike BigCo roles where you wait years to own a feature.
We ship weekly to 2M users and have promoted 6 engineers internally in the last 12 months.

---

Post-completion checklist:
[ ] Every specific claim (L&D budget, promotion count) is verifiable from internal data
[ ] Statement fits in one spoken sentence (recruiter phone screen test)
[ ] No adjectives: "innovative", "passionate", "world-class", "amazing"
[ ] Reviewed by at least 5 current employees before external use
