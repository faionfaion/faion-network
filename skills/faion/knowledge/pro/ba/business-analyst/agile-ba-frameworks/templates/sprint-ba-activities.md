# Sprint [N] BA Activities: [Team Name]

## Pre-Sprint (Refinement)

- [ ] Refine top [N] backlog items to "ready" state
- [ ] Write acceptance criteria for stories entering next sprint
- [ ] Create models or wireframes for complex stories
- [ ] Identify and document dependencies with other teams or systems

## Sprint Planning

- [ ] Present refined stories and confirm acceptance criteria with team
- [ ] Answer clarification questions during planning
- [ ] Document sprint commitment including any BA-owned tasks
- [ ] Flag any stories that are not ready and need additional refinement

## During Sprint

- [ ] Attend daily standup
- [ ] Respond to clarification requests within 30 minutes during working hours
- [ ] Review prototypes or designs when available
- [ ] Prepare backlog items for the next sprint (2 sprints ahead)

## Sprint Review

- [ ] Validate that acceptance criteria are met for each completed story
- [ ] Capture structured stakeholder feedback (distinguish "must fix" from "nice to have")
- [ ] Update backlog with new or modified stories from feedback

## Sprint Retrospective

- [ ] Share observations about BA process improvements
- [ ] Identify collaboration gaps (stories arriving without enough detail, late clarifications)
- [ ] Document agreed changes to BA process for next sprint
