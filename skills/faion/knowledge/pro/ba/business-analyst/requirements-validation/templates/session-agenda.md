## Ground Rules

- One requirement at a time — no skipping ahead.
- Issues are recorded, not debated — long discussions are parked for offline resolution.
- Severity is assigned by impact: H = blocks design, M = needs clarification before implementation, L = wording.

## Outputs

- Completed review checklist with issue log.
- Assigned resolution owners with agreed-upon resolution dates.
- Session minutes committed to git within 24 hours.
