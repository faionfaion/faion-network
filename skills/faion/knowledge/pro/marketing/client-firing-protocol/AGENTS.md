# Client Firing Protocol

## Summary

**One-sentence:** Criteria, transition plan, contract clean-exit, and post-mortem for firing a toxic agency client — the methodology the corpus has never offered.

**One-paragraph:** The corpus teaches how to win and serve clients but contains no methodology for firing one. Micro-agency margin is destroyed by 1-2 toxic clients more often than by underpricing. Mechanism: explicit firing criteria checklist, 30-60 day transition plan, contract-exit clauses, post-mortem to avoid the same pattern. Output: firing decision record + transition plan + post-mortem.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- agency or freelancer with ≥3 active clients
- one client meeting ≥2 firing criteria (scope abuse, payment delays, abusive behavior, mission-mismatch)
- founder has authority to end the relationship

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- single-client business — firing without replacement plan is suicide; build pipeline first
- client is in regulated/litigation context — defer to legal counsel
- personal relationship blockers — recuse to a co-founder if available

## Prerequisites

- active contract with termination clause read recently
- list of work-in-progress + handover artefacts
- 30-60 day runway to absorb revenue loss OR replacement pipeline
