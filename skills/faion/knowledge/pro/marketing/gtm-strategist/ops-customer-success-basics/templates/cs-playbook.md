# Customer Success Playbook: [Product Name]

## Success Definition
Our customers are successful when they: [primary outcome]
Measured by: [north-star metric] — target: [X]

## Onboarding (Day 1-30)

| Day | Trigger | Action | Owner | Channel |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Signup | Welcome email + resource links | Auto | Email |
| 3 | No key action | Check-in: need help? | Auto | Email |
| 7 | Time | Feature tip (tied to their use case) | Auto | Email |
| 14 | Time | Progress review (if < 50% adoption) | CSM | Email |
| 30 | Time | Success review call | CSM | Call |

## Ongoing Engagement

| Trigger | Action | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Health drops to yellow | Outreach with specific tip | CSM |
| Health drops to red | Personal call within 24h | CSM |
| New feature release | Targeted announcement | Auto |
| Usage milestone | Celebration + next goal | Auto |
| Renewal 30 days out | Pre-renewal check-in | CSM |
| Approaching plan limit | Upgrade offer | Auto |

## At-Risk Intervention

| Signal | Week 1 Action | Week 2 Action | Week 3 Escalation |
|--------|--------------|---------------|-------------------|
| No login 7d | Re-engagement email | — | — |
| No login 14d | Personal email from CSM | — | — |
| Health red | Personal email | Call | Founder outreach |
| Support escalation | Acknowledge + call | — | — |

**Cap: max 1 automated re-engagement per week per account. Pause after 3 automated touches without response.**

## Expansion Triggers

| Signal | Opportunity | Action |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Hitting plan limits | Upgrade | Proactive offer before blockage |
| Adding team members | More seats | Invite prompt |
| Using all advanced features | Premium tier | Feature showcase |
| Success milestone | Cross-sell | Related product mention |
