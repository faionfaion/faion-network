# Influencer Marketing

## Summary

**One-sentence:** Four-phase influencer framework: discover micro-influencers (10K–100K) by engagement quality, vet for audience match and fraud signals, personalized outreach, FTC-compliant disclosures, track ROI.

**One-paragraph:** Four-phase influencer framework: discover micro-influencers (10K–100K) by engagement quality, vet for audience match and fraud signals, personalized outreach, FTC-compliant disclosures, track ROI.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Reaching new audiences when SEO and paid ads have plateaued or have unfavorable unit economics.
- Launching a product to a niche where 10K–100K creators drive purchase decisions (DTC, SaaS prosumer, creator tools).
- Building a UGC pipeline cheaply via gifting + reuse rights instead of in-house production.
- Pre-launch seeding: placing product with micro creators in target niche to generate D-day content.

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- B2B enterprise (ACV above $10K) where buying committees ignore creator endorsements.
- Highly regulated verticals (medical, financial advice, securities) — FTC + sector compliance rules are brittle.
- Marketing budgets under $5K — fixed costs of vetting, outreach, and contracts erode ROI; start with referral programs instead.
- Products with no demonstrable 30-second hero moment — creators cannot make compelling content.
