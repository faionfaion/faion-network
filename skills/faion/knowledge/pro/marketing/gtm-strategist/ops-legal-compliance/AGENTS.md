# Legal Compliance

## Summary

An index and routing methodology for online-business legal compliance. Covers business structure selection, essential policies (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Refund), key regulations (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, COPPA, ADA, PCI-DSS), intellectual property protection, and contractor/vendor contracts. The actual content is split into two focused sub-methodologies: `ops-legal-basics` (foundation and policy framework) and `ops-legal-compliance-checklist` (stage-by-stage implementation tracker).

## Why

Legal exposure compounds silently. A founder without proper entity structure carries personal liability; a product without a GDPR-compliant privacy policy risks fines and EU market block; policies that drift from actual data practices create litigation surface. Compliance is cheapest when built in from day one, not retrofitted after an incident.

## When To Use

- Setting up legal foundation for a new business (entity, EIN, policies)
- Choosing business structure (LLC vs S-Corp vs sole prop)
- Drafting or auditing policies (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Refund)
- Pre-launch compliance review
- Routing an agent to the right sub-methodology (basics vs checklist)

## When NOT To Use

- Final production policy generation — LLM drafts require attorney review; this methodology provides structure, not approved legal text
- Jurisdiction-specific advice (non-US: UK GDPR, LGPD, India DPDP) — US-centric framing; localize with counsel
- Incident response / breach notification — legal counsel + privacy officer own this; agent role is logistics only
- Regulatory filings or correspondence with government bodies — human-only
