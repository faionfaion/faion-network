# Hook Sentence Template

Fill in one row per core product flow. Missing components are candidates for design intervention.

When user [TRIGGER],
they [ACTION] to get [REWARD],
which causes them to [INVEST],
creating the next [TRIGGER].

---

Example — Duolingo:
When user sees daily reminder (TRIGGER),
they complete a lesson (ACTION) to maintain streak (REWARD),
which increases their skill level and streak counter (INVEST),
making tomorrow's reminder more compelling (next TRIGGER).

---

Trigger types:
  External: push notification, email, SMS, calendar reminder, social mention
  Internal: boredom, loneliness, uncertainty, FOMO, routine cue

Variable reward types:
  Tribe: likes, comments, follows, social validation
  Hunt: scrolling for content, discovering new items
  Self: level-up, streak, achievement, mastery

Investment types:
  Data entry → personalization
  Following/connecting → relevant content
  Content creation → platform commitment
  Reputation/score → social capital at stake
  Customization → switching cost
  Learning curve → mastery investment
