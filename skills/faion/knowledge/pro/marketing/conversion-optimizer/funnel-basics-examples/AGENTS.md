# Funnel Optimization Examples and Benchmarks

## Summary

Concrete before/after case studies across SaaS, e-commerce, mobile app, B2B lead gen, and subscription funnels, paired with industry conversion benchmarks by step. Use to calibrate whether your funnel is performing at good/great level and to identify proven fixes for common drop-off patterns.

## Why

Optimization decisions made without benchmarks lead to optimizing steps that are already performing well. This reference anchors diagnosis — if your SaaS visitor-to-signup is 8%, it is already "great"; if it is 1%, the gap is 2-4x and warrants immediate attention.

## When To Use

- Benchmarking current funnel metrics against industry standards
- Identifying which common issues map to observed drop-off patterns
- Looking for proven solution patterns before generating new hypotheses
- Communicating optimization potential to stakeholders with real-world uplift data

## When NOT To Use

- Your product category is not represented (niche B2B, marketplace, etc.) — benchmarks may not apply
- Using benchmarks as targets without accounting for your audience and traffic quality
- Substituting case study results for your own A/B test data
