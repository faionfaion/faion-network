## Welcome (5 min)

"Welcome everyone. I'm [name] and I'll be leading our discussion today about [topic]. There are no right or wrong answers — I want to hear your genuine experiences and opinions. Please speak one at a time so we can capture everything."

**Ground rules:**
1. One person speaks at a time
2. All opinions are valuable — disagreement is welcome
3. What's said here stays here (confidentiality)
4. No phones during the session
5. Recording notice: [state recording consent]

## Warm-Up (10 min)

"Let's go around. Tell us your name and [easy question related to topic — e.g., how long you've been using this type of product]."

## Concept Reaction (15 min) — Reveal at 65-min mark only

**Materials to show:** [mockup / prototype / copy / naming options]

"I'm going to show you [concept]. Take a moment to look at it, then share your first reaction — don't edit yourself."

Questions:
- "What's your first impression?"
- "What do you like? What concerns you?"
- "Would you use this? Why or why not?"
- "[Name], you haven't spoken yet — what do you think?" [target quiet participant at 75-min mark]

## Wrap-Up (5 min)

"Before we finish:
- Is there anything we didn't discuss that you wanted to say?
- What's the one thing you want us to remember from today?"

"Thank you all — your time and honesty are genuinely valuable to us."
