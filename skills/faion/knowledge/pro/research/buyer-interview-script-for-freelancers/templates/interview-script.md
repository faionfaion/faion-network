# Buyer Interview Script — v1 (Freelancer)

## Section 1 — Warmup
- Walk me through the last freelancer you hired.

## Section 2 — Hiring Trigger
- What event made you start looking?
- When (date)?
- Who in your org made the call?

## Section 3 — Budget Authority
- Who signs off on freelance budget >$5k?

## Section 4 — Vendor Preference (last 3 hires)
- How did you find each one?
  - Referral / RFP / inbound search / other:
- What made you pick them over alternatives?

## Section 5 — Switching Pain (concrete cost only)
- Last time you replaced a freelancer, what did it cost you in hours, dollars, deals lost?
- If hypothetical, label as hypothetical.

## Rules
- No 'would you pay' phrasing.
- No pitching during interview.
