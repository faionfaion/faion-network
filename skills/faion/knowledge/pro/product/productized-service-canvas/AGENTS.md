# Productized Service Canvas

## Summary
A single page that converts a bespoke recurring engagement into a productized offer: one named outcome, one fixed price, one fixed timeline, an explicit exclusions list, a repeatable SOP, and the proof you have already delivered the outcome. Outcome: a sellable offer page draft, a delivery SOP, and a go/no-go signal — replacing SaaS-shaped MVP frameworks that do not fit service businesses.

## Applies If
- You sell time-and-materials or hourly today and want a fixed-scope offer
- You have delivered the same outcome ≥3 times for previous clients
- You can describe the outcome in one client-language sentence
- You can name the buyer (role + company size + trigger event)

## Skip If
- You have not yet delivered the outcome for a paying client
- The outcome is too custom to standardize (every engagement diverges)
- You are not the bottleneck in delivery (this is a SaaS, use MVP frameworks instead)
- The buyer cares about hourly rate, not outcome (wrong buyer for a productized offer)
