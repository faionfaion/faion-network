# 30-min Freelancer Discovery Call

**Prospect:** `<name>` — `<company>` — `<role>`
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM

## 1. Context (5 min)

- Walk me through what your team does today.
- How did you find me?
- What made you reach out now?

## 2. Pain (8 min)

- What's slowing you down most this quarter?
- Tell me about the last time it broke.
- Walk me through what happens when it happens.

## 3. Success criteria (5 min)

- What would "done" look like in 90 days?
- How will you measure it?
- What does a good day with this look like?

## 4. Budget signals (5 min)

- What are you spending on this today?
- What budget have you allocated?
- What's the cost of NOT fixing this?

## 5. Decision process (5 min)

- Who else weighs in?
- What's your timeline to start?
- What would prevent you from moving forward?

## Close (2 min)

- Next step: 1-page proposal within 24h.
- Follow-up: schedule before ending the call.
