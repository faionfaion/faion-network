# Kickoff Interview Script

Consent: "Can I record this 60-minute conversation for project documentation? You can review the transcript."

1. In one sentence, what is the project trying to achieve?
2. Who is the named owner of the primary outcome on the client side?
3. What artefacts (docs, tickets, decks) should I read first?
4. Which three stakeholders should I talk to in the first week?
5. What does "done" look like at the end of this engagement?
6. What is the hardest part of this project from your seat?
7. Which terms inside this project domain confuse people most?
8. Which existing process is most affected by what we are building?
9. What past attempts have been made? Why did they stop or fail?
10. What is the explicit out-of-scope?
11. Who approves scope changes? How fast can they sign?
12. What would make you, in 6 months, consider this engagement a success?
