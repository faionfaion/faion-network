# Mentor weekly review — 30 min

## Before the call (5 min)
- [ ] Read junior's reflection for this week.
- [ ] Open the exercise diff / output in the IDE.

## During (20 min)
- [ ] Ask junior to walk you through their reflection (5 min).
- [ ] Identify 1 hallucination, 1 well-scoped edit, 1 risk in the work (10 min).
- [ ] Score the week's rubric items pass/fail with one-line evidence note (5 min).

## After (5 min)
- [ ] Append scored items + talking points for next week to curriculum runbook.
- [ ] Flag if 2 consecutive weeks have failed rubric items → trigger remediation.

## Red flags to escalate
- Junior cannot explain code they committed.
- Junior bypassed the approved-tool list.
- Junior pasted credentials or PII into a prompt at any point.
- Junior reflection sounds verbatim AI-generated.
