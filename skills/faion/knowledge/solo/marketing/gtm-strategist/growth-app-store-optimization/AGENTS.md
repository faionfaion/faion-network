# App Store Optimization (ASO)

## Summary

**One-sentence:** ASO is the discipline of improving app visibility in Apple App Store and Google Play through metadata, visual assets, and review strategy.

**One-paragraph:** ASO is the discipline of improving app visibility in Apple App Store and Google Play through metadata, visual assets, and review strategy. It splits into two levers: visibility (keyword ranking) and conversion (listing page to download). Both must be optimized together. Organic search drives 65%+ of downloads, making ASO the highest-leverage discovery channel for mobile apps.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Launching a new mobile app and setting up the store listing for the first time
- Organic downloads have plateaued despite active UA spend
- Conversion rate from store page view to download is below 30%
- Expanding into new language markets (localization)
- After a major product update that adds keyword-relevant features

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- Web apps, PWAs, or desktop software — wrong channel entirely
- App has fewer than 50 weekly organic impressions — too early; fix product-market fit first
- B2B enterprise apps distributed via MDM — discovery is sales-led, not store-driven
- When budget for new screenshots/video does not exist — visual improvement is the highest-ROI ASO lever and skipping it limits results
