# Growth App Store Optimization - Examples

### Before/After Optimization

**Before:**
```
Name: MyApp
Subtitle: The best app

```

**After:**
```

Subtitle: To-do lists & productivity

Rating: 4.6 stars (500 reviews)
```

**Results:**
- 3x increase in organic downloads
- 40% higher conversion rate
- Top 10 for "todo list" keyword
