# LinkedIn Connection Request

Keep to 300 characters (LinkedIn limit). No pitch. Establish shared context.

---

Hey [Name] — came across your work on [specific thing: post, project, company].
Building something in [related space] and would love to connect.

[Your first name]

---

# LinkedIn Message After Connection

Send 24–48 hours after connection is accepted. One topic. Soft CTA.

---

Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

Noticed you're leading [initiative / role] at [Company]. We've been helping [similar companies or role types] with [specific challenge] — curious if that's on your radar?

Either way, glad to be connected.

[Your name]

---

Notes:
- Never pitch in the connection request itself
- Wait for acceptance before sending any follow-up
- If no reply to first message after 7 days: send one more message with a different angle, then stop
- LinkedIn InMail for non-connections: same format, slightly more context on who you are
