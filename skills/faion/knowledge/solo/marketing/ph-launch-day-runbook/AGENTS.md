# PH Launch Day Runbook

## Summary
An hour-by-hour, PT-anchored runbook for Product Hunt launch day. Owns the moments where indies most often fumble — the comment-back ritual, the upvote-conversation-to-trial conversion, the post-12h energy crash. Outcome: launch-day execution becomes a checklist (set timers, follow scripts, log conversions) so the founder can stay heads-down on the hunters and ship a #1-of-day attempt without improvising on the day.

## Applies If
- You have a Product Hunt launch scheduled within 14 days
- You have prepared the 4-week prep deck (hunter, assets, hype list)
- You are launching solo or with one helper, not a 5-person team
- You can clear your calendar for ≥14 hours on launch day

## Skip If
- You are doing a quiet stealth launch (no scoreboard pressure)
- You are launching as a side-experiment with no go-to-market goal
- You have a paid agency running the launch (give them this runbook)
- You have not done prep — this is the day-of runbook, not the prep guide
