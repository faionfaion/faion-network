# Topical Authority

## Summary

**One-sentence:** Build topical authority by covering an entire subject comprehensively through topic clusters: one pillar page (broad overview) plus 8-30 supporting subtopic articles, all internally linked, refreshed at least every 6 months.

**One-paragraph:** Build topical authority by covering an entire subject comprehensively through topic clusters: one pillar page (broad overview) plus 8-30 supporting subtopic articles, all internally linked, refreshed at least every 6 months. Google's helpful-content and semantic-search systems reward sites that demonstrate end-to-end domain expertise over those targeting isolated keywords.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Launching a new content area and wanting to establish authority within 3-6 months
- Existing content exists but rankings have plateaued despite on-page optimisation
- Building a pillar-cluster strategy from scratch in a vertical with strong incumbents
- Evaluating which topics to invest in based on authority-building ROI
- AI Overviews / generative-search citations are the primary traffic goal
- Trigger phrase from user: "rankings flat", "competitor outranks us", "build authority", "pillar page", "content cluster", "topic map"

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- Single-page or micro-sites with no room for content clusters
- Bottleneck is technical SEO, not content depth — fix crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals first
- Time-sensitive news/event content where freshness, not depth, drives rankings
- Local-intent SERPs dominated by Google Business Profile (use local SEO methodology)
- Domain has DR < 10 and no backlinks — invest in foundational link-building first
