# Zero-Click Search Adaptation

## Summary

**One-sentence:** Optimize for citation-based visibility in AI Overviews (cited brands earn +35% clicks) instead of raw traffic — 83% of AIO-served searches end without a click.

**One-paragraph:** Optimize for citation-based visibility in AI Overviews (cited brands earn +35% clicks) instead of raw traffic — 83% of AIO-served searches end without a click.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Trigger: GSC shows impressions stable or growing while clicks drop more than 20% over 90 days for top-50 queries — almost always AI Overview suppression.
- Trigger: site experienced significant traffic drop after Google AI Overview rollout in the niche.
- Building a new content strategy that must account for AI search landscape.
- Deciding which content types to invest in for 2026+ SEO.
- Redefining SEO KPIs and reporting to include AI visibility metrics.

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- Brand new site with no technical foundation — fix crawlability and content first.
- When traffic volume is still growing — if AI Overviews have not impacted your niche, do not over-optimize for zero-click yet.
- For local SEO (maps/places) — zero-click dynamics differ significantly there.
