# Post Launch Conversion Drip

## Summary
A four-email drip designed specifically for the post-launch traffic spike (PH, HN, newsletter blast, viral tweet). Each email has a distinct job: T+0 set expectation, T+2 deliver an aha-moment, T+7 prove with case-study, T+14 ask for a decision. Outcome: a copy-pasteable drip pack that captures the 70% of launch-week signups that otherwise vanish in the first two weeks.

## Applies If
- You ran a high-volume launch (PH, HN front-page, viral thread) in the last 7 days
- You collected ≥100 emails or signups during/after the launch
- You have basic email automation (ConvertKit, Loops, Resend, Mailchimp)
- You can compose 4 emails plus 1 follow-up sequence

## Skip If
- Your spike was <50 signups (sample too small to justify the drip)
- You already have a mature onboarding sequence with launch tagging (extend it)
- The product has no email-relevant value (instant-use anonymous tools)
- You launch every two weeks (run a recurring drip, not a one-off pack)
