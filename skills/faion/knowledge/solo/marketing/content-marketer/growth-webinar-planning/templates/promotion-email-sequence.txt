# Webinar Promotion Email Sequence (3 emails)

## Email 1 — Day -14 (Announcement)
Subject: [You're invited:] [Webinar Title] on [Date]
Preview: Learn [specific outcome] in 60 minutes — free live training

Body (150 words):
Hey [Name],

I'm running a free live training on [date] at [time] [timezone].

Topic: [Webinar Title]

You'll learn:
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]
- [Takeaway 3]

[Who it's for: 1 sentence]

Register here (free): [LINK]

[Name]

P.S. Seats are limited. Register now to get the replay even if you can't attend live.

---

## Email 2 — Day -7 (Value Preview)
Subject: What I'll share on [date] (sneak peek)
Preview: One of the 3 frameworks I'm teaching live next week

Body: Share one specific tip or insight from the webinar. Prove the value. CTA to register.

---

## Email 3 — Day -1 (Final Reminder)
Subject: Tomorrow at [time] — are you registered?
Preview: Last chance to grab your spot for [Webinar Title]

Body: Urgency, date/time reminder, 3 benefit bullets, register link, replay promise.
