# Email Marketing

## Summary

**One-sentence:** A value-first email marketing framework covering list building, welcome sequences, newsletter strategy, segmentation, and sales email sequences.

**One-paragraph:** A value-first email marketing framework covering list building, welcome sequences, newsletter strategy, segmentation, and sales email sequences. Never purchase or rent lists — spam complaints above 0.1% trigger Google/Yahoo bulk sender sanctions. Build organically with lead magnets; automate the welcome sequence before any broadcast.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Launching a new product and building an email list from scratch
- Setting up automated welcome and nurture sequences for an existing audience
- Converting social media followers to an owned channel
- Running a promotional campaign (product launch, sale, cohort open)
- Re-engaging an inactive or cold subscriber list

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- Audience is still undefined — list-building without ICP clarity creates churn
- Fewer than 50 subscribers with no consistent content cadence — focus on content first
- Pure B2B enterprise with procurement-driven sales — email alone won't close
- Compliance requirements prevent automated outreach (certain financial or healthcare contexts)
