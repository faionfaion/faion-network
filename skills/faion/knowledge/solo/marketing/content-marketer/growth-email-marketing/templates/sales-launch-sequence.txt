# 8-Day Product Launch Email Sequence

## Day -7: Announcement + Value Preview
Subject: Something big is coming on [date]
Body: Tease the product. Give one valuable insight related to the problem it solves.
     End with: "More on [date]."

## Day -3: Deep Dive on the Problem
Subject: The real reason [pain point] is so hard to fix
Body: Detailed breakdown of the problem. Empathy-first. No mention of product yet.
     End: "I'll show you how I solved this on [date]."

## Day -1: Early Bird Reminder
Subject: [Product] launches tomorrow — early bird closes at midnight
Body: Remind about launch. Early bird offer or bonus. Create urgency without false scarcity.

## Day 0: LAUNCH
Subject: [Product] is live — here's everything you need to know
Body: Full details. Price. What's included. Testimonials if available. Strong CTA.
     Include link 3+ times in the email.

## Day 1: FAQ / Objection Handling
Subject: "Is [Product] right for me?" — answering your questions
Body: Answer the top 3-5 objections. Use real questions if you have them.

## Day 3: Case Study / Testimonial
Subject: How [Customer] achieved [Specific Result] with [Product]
Body: Specific story with numbers. End with CTA.

## Day 5: Last Chance / Scarcity
Subject: [X] hours left — [Product] closes [date]
Body: Genuine scarcity (price increase, bonus removal, or enrollment close). CTA.

## Day 6: Final Reminder (Morning of close)
Subject: Closing tonight at [time] — last chance
Body: Short. Urgency. One CTA. No more than 100 words.
