# 5-Email Welcome Sequence

## Email 1 — Day 0 (Immediate on signup)
Subject: Your [Lead Magnet] is here
Preview text: Plus a quick intro from me

Hey [Name],

Welcome! Here's your [Lead Magnet]: [LINK]

Quick intro: I'm [Name], and I help [ICP] achieve [specific outcome].

Over the next few days, I'll share:
- [Value 1]
- [Value 2]
- [Value 3]

For now, download your [Lead Magnet] and let me know what you think by replying to this email.

[Name]

P.S. [Quick tip related to the lead magnet]

---

## Email 2 — Day 1
Subject: Why I created [Product/Brand]
Body: Personal story → philosophy → what you stand for → builds connection

---

## Email 3 — Day 3
Subject: [Benefit-driven subject line — not generic]
Body: Link to your best existing content piece (blog post, video, case study).
      Add 2-3 sentences of context on why this piece matters for their situation.

---

## Email 4 — Day 5
Subject: How [Customer Name or Type] achieved [Specific Result]
Body: Case study or testimonial with specific numbers.
      End with: "You can do the same. Here's how: [brief path]"

---

## Email 5 — Day 7
Subject: Ready to [achieve outcome]?
Body: Soft mention of your product/service.
     No hard sell. Offer the next logical step.
     "When you're ready: [LINK]"
