# Search Everywhere Optimization (GEO / AEO)

## Summary

Multi-platform visibility strategy targeting Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, YouTube,
and TikTok simultaneously. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) focuses on being the
source AI engines cite; AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content so AI
systems extract and surface it. The core rule: every H2/H3 heading should be a
question answered directly in the first paragraph beneath it.

## Why

Traditional SEO targets only Google ranked links. LLM-powered engines (ChatGPT,
Perplexity) now handle 400M+ weekly queries and are projected to overtake traditional
search by 2027. Content that AI engines cannot extract and cite loses share regardless
of Google rank. Content updated within 30 days gets 3.2x more AI citations (Semrush
research).

## When To Use

- Publishing content that should be cited by AI engines in addition to ranking in Google
- Auditing existing content library for GEO/AEO readiness
- Creating new content targeted at AI Overview inclusion or featured snippets
- Building brand entity presence across the web (schema, consistent mentions)
- Monitoring AI share of voice and citation frequency for a brand or product

## When NOT To Use

- Purely transactional/local content (restaurant menu, service area page) — local SEO dominates
- Audience is exclusively on Amazon or TikTok — GEO/AEO has no lift there
- Domain authority is near-zero — AI engines won't cite content without established presence
- No content publishing cadence — GEO is a content strategy layer, not a substitute for content
