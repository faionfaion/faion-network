# Prompt: Generate 5 Hook Variants per Formula Type

Generate 5 hook variants for a TikTok video on the topic: "[TOPIC]"
Target audience: [ICP]
Product context: [PRODUCT ONE-LINER]

Each hook:
- One formula type (see list below)
- Spoken text: max 10 words
- On-screen text: max 6 words (can differ from spoken for emphasis)
- Must create urgency or pattern interruption — not just describe the topic

Formula types to cover:
1. curiosity: creates an information gap the viewer must resolve
2. challenge: contradicts something the viewer currently does or believes
3. promise: states a specific result the viewer wants
4. relatability: mirrors a situation the viewer recognizes immediately
5. controversy: states an unpopular opinion the viewer will react to

Output as JSON array:
[
  {"formula": "curiosity", "spoken": "...", "on_screen": "..."},
  {"formula": "challenge", "spoken": "...", "on_screen": "..."},
  {"formula": "promise", "spoken": "...", "on_screen": "..."},
  {"formula": "relatability", "spoken": "...", "on_screen": "..."},
  {"formula": "controversy", "spoken": "...", "on_screen": "..."}
]
