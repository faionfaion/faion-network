# TikTok Growth Strategies

## Summary

Advanced TikTok tactics for accounts past the setup phase: hook formula mastery, algorithm signal optimization, posting cadence, trend usage, conversion funnel, and growth loops (duets, stitches, series cliffhangers). The core rule: watch-time optimization outperforms posting frequency — 3 videos at 70%+ completion rate outperform 7 videos at 40% completion in algorithm distribution.

## Why

The TikTok For You Page algorithm weights watch time and completion rate above all other signals. Accounts that optimize hooks and structure before scaling frequency grow faster with less effort. Hook formulas (curiosity, challenge, promise, relatability, controversy) produce measurably different results per niche; testing 5 variants before scaling is the minimum valid sample size.

## When To Use

- Generating hook variants (5+ per video) in different formula types for A/B testing
- Analyzing watch-time drop-off patterns and recommending structural changes
- Drafting trend adaptation scripts given a specific sound/format and niche context
- Creating duet/stitch strategy briefs for competitor or niche creator engagement
- Generating cliffhanger series arcs ("Part 1"/"Part 2") for series content strategy

## When NOT To Use

- Real-time trend identification — agents cannot browse the TikTok Discover or For You Page
- Selecting which trending sounds to use — sound trend data expires in 24-48 hours
- Predicting which specific video will go viral
- Running TikTok Ads campaigns — paid media task with separate workflow
- Any automated posting — violates ToS and risks account ban
