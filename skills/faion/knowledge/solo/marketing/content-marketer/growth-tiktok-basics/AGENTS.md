# TikTok Basics & Setup

## Summary

Account setup and content strategy foundation for TikTok. Covers profile optimization (Creator vs Business account), content pillars for B2B/SaaS, video formats, and a phased ramp plan (weeks 1 → months 3+). The core rule: hook in the first 1-3 seconds determines whether the algorithm amplifies a video, making hook quality the highest-leverage variable before any other optimization.

## Why

TikTok delivers the highest organic reach of any social platform (1.5B+ MAU). Businesses that post corporate-style content fail; those who match the platform's native style — educational, authentic, trend-aware — can acquire customers at near-zero CAC. The HOOK→RETAIN→CONVERT→COMPOUND pattern is how the For You Page algorithm works: watch time and completion rate drive distribution.

## When To Use

- Launching a TikTok presence from zero for a B2B/SaaS or solo product
- Writing video script outlines (educational, build-in-public, demo formats)
- Drafting bio copy and profile optimization for a new account
- Creating a 30-day content calendar across 3-4 defined pillars
- Analyzing competitor TikTok accounts to extract content patterns before launch
- Writing caption variants with different hooks for A/B testing

## When NOT To Use

- Filming, editing, or uploading video — agents cannot operate mobile apps or video software
- Deciding whether TikTok is the right channel without audience data
- Generating trend-based content — trends expire in hours; training data is too stale
- Accounts targeting audiences in countries with TikTok restrictions
- Any automated posting workflow — violates TikTok ToS
