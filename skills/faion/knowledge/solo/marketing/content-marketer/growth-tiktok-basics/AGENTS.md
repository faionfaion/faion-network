# TikTok Basics & Setup

## Summary

**One-sentence:** TikTok account setup and content strategy for B2B/SaaS.

**One-paragraph:** TikTok account setup and content strategy for B2B/SaaS. Hook quality in the first 1-3 seconds is the highest-leverage variable. Creator accounts at launch, 3-4 content pillars, daily posting for 4 weeks, then scale to 3-5 videos/day.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- Launching a TikTok presence from zero for a B2B/SaaS or solo product.
- Writing video script outlines (educational, build-in-public, demo formats).
- Drafting bio copy and profile optimization for a new account.
- Creating a 30-day content calendar across 3-4 defined pillars.
- Analyzing competitor TikTok accounts to extract content patterns before launch.
- Writing caption variants with different hooks for A/B testing.

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- Filming, editing, or uploading video — agents cannot operate mobile apps or video software.
- Deciding whether TikTok is the right channel without audience data.
- Generating trend-based content — trends expire in hours; training data is too stale.
- Accounts targeting audiences in countries with TikTok restrictions.
- Any automated posting workflow — violates TikTok ToS.
