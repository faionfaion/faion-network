# Landing Page Copy Structure

## Headline
[Biggest benefit in one line — use one of: How-to, Number, Question, Result-without-pain, Stop X]

## Subheadline
[Expand on headline, clarify who it's for, add one more detail]

## Hero Section
[1-2 sentences describing the pain point you solve — empathy first]
[CTA Button: [Action verb] + [specific benefit] — e.g., "Start My Free Trial"]

## Problem Section (Optional — for high-awareness ICP)
[Describe their current situation in their language. Do not mention your product yet.]

## Solution Section
[Introduce your product as the logical answer to the problem above]
[One sentence: "[Product] is [category] that [outcome] for [ICP]"]

## Benefits (3-5 bullet points)
### [Benefit headline 1]
[Feature] means [specific benefit for them]. [Proof if available]

### [Benefit headline 2]
[Feature] means [specific benefit for them].

### [Benefit headline 3]
[Feature] means [specific benefit for them].

## Social Proof
[Testimonials with name, role, and specific result — not generic praise]
[Logos of known customers or publications if applicable]
[Number: "Trusted by X [ICP role]"]

## How It Works (Optional)
1. [Step 1 — action they take]
2. [Step 2 — what happens]
3. [Step 3 — the outcome they get]

## Objection Handling
[Address the top 2 objections using FAQ format or inline copy]

## Final CTA Section
[Restate the key benefit in 1 sentence]
[CTA Button: same as hero or progressed ("Get Instant Access")]

## Risk Reversal
[Guarantee, free trial, or return policy — reduce perceived risk]
[E.g., "30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked."]
