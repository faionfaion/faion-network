# Solo Niche Disqualifier Checklist

## Summary

**One-sentence:** A short list of hard disqualifier signals that lets a freelancer reject a cold lead in under 5 minutes, protecting the specialisation premium.

**One-paragraph:** Specialisation is what lets a freelancer command 2–4x generalist rates, but it only works if the freelancer says no to most inbound. Without a written disqualifier list, every lead becomes a 30-minute discovery call that ends in a polite decline — a per-week productivity tax. This checklist names the signals (wrong industry, sub-floor rate, brief under 100 words, founder cannot identify the decision-maker, AI-spam outreach) that let the freelancer reply "not a fit" in one paragraph.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- the freelancer has a stated specialisation (vertical, technical, or buyer-persona)
- inbound exceeds capacity (>2 cold replies / week)
- a floor rate is defined (in $ / hour or $ / project)
- LinkedIn / email cold inbound is the primary channel

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- the freelancer is in capacity-building mode and wants any paying client
- specialisation is not yet defined — generalists need a positioning methodology, not a filter
- inbound is referral-only (already pre-qualified by source)

## Prerequisites

- written specialisation statement ("I do X for Y companies")
- documented floor rate
- one-paragraph "not a fit" reply template
