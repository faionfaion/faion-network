# Hook Bank Template

## Summary
A versioned library of hook patterns and concrete instances tagged by format, platform, and observed result. The bank is filled in over weeks of posting and re-read every time a new post is drafted, so the writer starts from a working hook instead of a blank page. Outcome: a personal swipe file that turns "what do I tweet?" from a 20-minute paralysis into a 3-minute remix of a proven hook.

## Applies If
- You post on X / IH / LinkedIn / threads at least 3×/week
- You have shipped ≥30 posts and can label each as flopped/average/spiked
- Daily writing time is your bottleneck, not idea generation
- You want to optimize hook quality, not topic generation

## Skip If
- You post fewer than 3×/week (sample size too small to learn from)
- You write only long-form (blogs, newsletters) where hooks are buried
- You delegate writing to a ghostwriter (give them the bank, do not use it yourself)
- You have not yet posted anything (use swipe-file-tweet-hooks for cold start)
