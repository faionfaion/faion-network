# LinkedIn Growth Strategy

## Summary

A systematic playbook for building B2B authority and generating inbound leads on LinkedIn. The four-stage cycle is: Optimize profile as a landing page → Create resonant content (personal stories, contrarian takes, how-to posts, carousels) → Engage via substantive daily commenting → Convert attention to business through lead magnets and DM sequences. Core rule: post 3-5 times per week and comment on 20+ posts daily — organic reach compounds only with consistent engagement.

## Why

LinkedIn's organic reach remains higher than any other B2B platform, but it rewards personal voice and authentic narratives, not corporate announcements. The algorithm surfaces posts with early engagement in the first hour; commenting strategy on high-follower posts drives profile discovery at lower cost than standalone posts. Profile optimization converts arriving visitors — a weak headline wastes the traffic that content generates.

## When To Use

- Building thought leadership and inbound pipeline for a B2B product or consultancy
- Launching a personal brand for a founder, consultant, or specialist
- Generating a 4-week content calendar with post types and schedule
- Auditing and rewriting a LinkedIn profile for conversion
- Analyzing engagement data from Shield or LinkedIn Analytics exports

## When NOT To Use

- Real-time engagement (commenting, replying) — requires human authenticity; automated comments are detectable and harm credibility
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator automation — violates LinkedIn ToS; accounts get banned
- Accounts with no defined content pillars or brand context — generic posts perform below average
- Replacing human relationship-building in DMs — drafts can be prepared but must be sent and adapted by a human
