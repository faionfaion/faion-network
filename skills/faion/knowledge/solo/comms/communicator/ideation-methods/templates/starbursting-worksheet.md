# Starbursting Worksheet: [IDEA/CONCEPT]

Generate at least 5 questions per category before answering any of them.

## WHO?
- Who are the primary users?
- Who pays?
- Who maintains/operates it?
- Who are the competitors?
- Who might oppose it?
-

## WHAT?
- What problem does it solve?
- What features are must-haves vs nice-to-haves?
- What are the success metrics?
- What are the risks?
- What resources are needed?
-

## WHERE?
- Where will this be used?
- Where is data stored?
- Where are the users located?
- Where do we sell/distribute?
-

## WHEN?
- When do users need this?
- When should we launch?
- When are key milestones?
- When will we break even?
-

## WHY?
- Why would users choose us over alternatives?
- Why now vs. later?
- Why would this fail?
- Why do we believe in this?
-

## HOW?
- How to acquire first users?
- How to monetize?
- How to measure success?
- How to scale?
- How to differentiate?
-
