Role: idea-scorer (separate context from idea-generator — do not reuse the generation context)

Input: [30 idea candidates JSON from idea-generator]

Score each idea on 5 weighted criteria (1-5):
- Market Size (20%): addressable market for this specific audience+problem
- Personal Fit (25%): founder's excitement and skill match — mark ALL as "founder_input_required", do NOT score
- Competition (15%): how crowded is the space for this specific positioning
- Monetization (20%): clarity of revenue path (model + price point)
- Speed to MVP (20%): realistic time to working prototype

Hard caps:
- Speed to MVP: max score 3 for any idea touching auth, billing, or third-party integrations
- Personal Fit: never scored by agent — always "founder_input_required"
- Market Size >= 4 or Competition >= 4: requires citation URL or flag as "unverified"

Mode-collapse detection: if more than 5 of your top-10 ideas have "AI" as the core mechanism, flag as potential mode-collapse and re-diversify bottom 5.

Output JSON:
[{
  "name": "...",
  "market_size": N,
  "personal_fit": "founder_input_required",
  "competition": N,
  "monetization": N,
  "speed_to_mvp": N,
  "weighted_total": N,  // exclude personal_fit from weighted total
  "citations": {"market_size": "URL or unverified", "competition": "URL or unverified"},
  "rationale": "one sentence summary of key signals"
}]
Sort by weighted_total descending. Return top 10 only.
