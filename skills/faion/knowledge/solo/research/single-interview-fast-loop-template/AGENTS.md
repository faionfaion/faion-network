# Single Interview Fast Loop Template

## Summary

**One-sentence:** A bounded loop for PMs and founders running ONE interview at a time: 30-minute structured prep before, 60-minute interview, 24-hour synthesis-and-share-out — designed so single interviews compound into evidence instead of evaporating.

**One-paragraph:** Existing interview playbooks assume batch studies (recruit 8, schedule a week, synthesize all together). PMs and solo founders increasingly run one interview when the opportunity appears — a churned customer, a prospect on a discovery call, a power user with 30 minutes free. The single-interview loop codifies a tight prep (one written hypothesis + 5 must-asks + a single decision the interview will inform) and a hard 24-hour synthesis deadline so the learning lands while context is hot.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- you have exactly one interview opportunity (no batch study)
- there is a specific decision or hypothesis this interview should inform
- the interviewer can commit 30 min prep + 60 min interview + 60 min synthesis inside ~36 hours total
- tier == solo or higher (gating enforced by tier-manifest)

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- a batch study is already running for the same question — fold this interview into it
- the interview is a sales call, not discovery — different playbook
- the interviewer cannot complete synthesis within 24 hours — defer until they can
