# UX Researcher Domain Skill

## Purpose

Solo-tier UX research for solopreneurs evaluating and improving their own products. Covers Nielsen heuristics, information architecture, journey mapping, usability and A/B testing, content audits, design critiques, mobile UX patterns, and competitive analysis. Larger enterprise UX research programs live in `faion-pro-ux-researcher`.
