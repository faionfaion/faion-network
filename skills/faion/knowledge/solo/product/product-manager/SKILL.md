# Product Manager Domain Skill

## Purpose

Solo-tier product management for solopreneurs running discovery, planning, and delivery alone. Covers continuous discovery, prioritisation (MoSCoW, RICE), MVP scoping, OKRs, outcome-based roadmaps, launches, spec writing, and user-story mapping. Larger enterprise PM (portfolio, multi-team alignment) lives in `faion-pro-product-manager`.
