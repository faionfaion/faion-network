# Burn Checklist

Tick every line that exists in your life; enter $0 only if it truly is.

## Personal
- [ ] Housing (rent / mortgage / utilities)
- [ ] Groceries + dining
- [ ] Health insurance (full premium, not just copay)
- [ ] Other insurance (life, disability, renters)
- [ ] Transportation (car payment / fuel / transit / rideshares)
- [ ] Childcare / education
- [ ] Subscriptions (streaming, gym, phone)
- [ ] Taxes set-aside (federal + state + self-employment)
- [ ] Retirement contributions
- [ ] Savings deposit (monthly target)

## Business
- [ ] Hosting + infra
- [ ] Tooling (SaaS subs the business depends on)
- [ ] Contractors / freelancers
- [ ] Marketing / ads
- [ ] Accounting / legal
- [ ] Business insurance
- [ ] Domain / SSL / minor fees

Sum personal → `burn_personal`. Sum business → `burn_business`. If anything is zero, justify in a one-line note.
