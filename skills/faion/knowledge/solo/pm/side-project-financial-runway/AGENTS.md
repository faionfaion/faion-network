# Side-Project Financial Runway

## Summary

**One-sentence:** Personal-runway math: monthly burn vs MRR ramp, when to leave a job, geo-arbitrage budget — life/business hybrid doc indie hackers need.

**One-paragraph:** ops-financial-planning exists under gtm-strategist but it's product-pricing oriented. Indie hackers need personal-runway math. Output: runway model + leave-job trigger + geo-arbitrage scenario.

## Applies If (ALL must hold)

- indie hacker working day-job while building side-SaaS
- ambition to go full-time on side product
- personal + business finances are realistic, not aspirational

## Skip If (ANY kills it)

- founder already full-time on the product
- side-project is hobby with no income goal
- founder has VC + unlimited runway (different math)

## Prerequisites

- monthly expenses (personal) + business expenses
- savings buffer + emergency fund
- current MRR + 6-month trend
