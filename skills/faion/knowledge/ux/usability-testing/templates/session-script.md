# Usability Test Script

## Introduction (5 min)

"Hi [name], thanks for joining today. I'm [name], and I'll be guiding you through this session.

We're testing [product], not testing you. There are no wrong answers. We want honest feedback to improve the product.

I'd like you to think aloud as you work — tell me what you're thinking, what you expect, and what confuses you.

Do you have any questions before we start?"

## Pre-test Questions (5 min)

1. What is your experience with [domain]?
2. Have you used similar products before?

## Tasks (30-45 min)

### Task 1

"Imagine you want to [scenario]. Starting from this screen, please [task]. Remember to think aloud."

[Observe and note without helping. Do not respond to "is this right?" except with "what do you think?"]

**Post-task questions:**
- On a scale of 1-5, how easy was that task?
- What was confusing, if anything?

### Task 2

[Same structure]

## Post-test Questions (10 min)

1. What was your overall impression of [product]?
2. What would you change if you could?
3. Would you use this product? Why or why not?

## Close

"Thank you so much for your time and feedback. Your input will directly improve the product. Do you have any final questions for me?"
