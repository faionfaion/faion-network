## Welcome and Ground Rules (5 min)

**Script:**
"Welcome everyone. I'm [name] and I'll be leading our discussion today about [topic].
There are no right or wrong answers — I want to hear all perspectives.
Please speak one at a time so we can capture everything.
Any questions before we start?"

**Ground rules (display on screen or print):**
1. One person speaks at a time
2. All opinions are valuable — disagreement is welcome
3. What's said here stays here
4. Phones away please

## Warm-Up (10 min)

"Let's go around. Tell us your name and [easy question related to topic]."
[Use an easy, non-threatening question to establish comfort and speaking turns]

## Topic 1: [Name] (20 min)

**Written first (2 min):** "Before we discuss, write one word or phrase that comes to mind when you think about [topic area]."
[Collect cards/screen submissions before anyone speaks]

**Opening question:** "[Broad, open question — no right answer]"

**Probes:**
- "Can you tell me more about that?"
- "Does anyone have a different experience?"
- "Why do you think that is?"
- "What would that look like for you?"

## Concept Reaction (15 min) — if showing stimuli

**Materials:** [What you are showing — screenshots, prototype, concept description]

"I'm going to show you [concept]. Take two minutes to look at it quietly, then share your first reactions."
[Silent review before discussion]

**Questions:**
- "What is your first impression?"
- "What would you say to a friend about this?"
- "What concerns or questions does it raise for you?"

---

## Wrap-Up (5 min)

"Before we finish:
- Is there anything we didn't discuss that you wanted to say?
- What's the one thing you want us to remember?"

"Thank you all for your time. [Compensation instructions.]"
