Interview opener sequence (Vision → Framing → Weakness → Pedestal → Ask):

1. Vision: "I'm trying to understand [one-sentence problem statement]."
2. Framing: "I have nothing to sell — I'm just here to learn from you."
3. Weakness: "Honestly, I don't know yet if this problem is as big as I think it is."
4. Pedestal: "You're one of the few people who really understands [their domain], so your perspective matters a lot."
5. Ask: "Could I take 20 minutes of your time to ask you a few questions about your experience with [topic]?"
