# Contextual Inquiry — <study name>

## Frame
You are the apprentice. User is the master. Never suggest.

## Opening (5 min)
- Confirm consent + recording
- Ask user to walk through current task in their own words

## During the work (60-150 min)
Observation prompts:
- What were you trying to do there?
- What did you expect to happen?
- Why did you choose that approach?
- What would you do if X happened?

Capture: verbatim quote, breakdown, workaround, artefact photo (with consent).

## Closing (5-10 min)
Clarify ambiguous moments; thank participant.
