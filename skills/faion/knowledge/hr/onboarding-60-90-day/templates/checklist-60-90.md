## Days 31-60: Contributing Phase

### Weeks 5-6: First Contributions

- [ ] First project scope defined and agreed with manager (carryover from day-30 review).
- [ ] Own first project actively in progress.
- [ ] Participating actively in recurring ceremonies (contributing, not just attending).
- [ ] Building cross-functional stakeholder relationships (target: 3-5 new contacts).
- [ ] Identified one area for process or practice improvement.

### Weeks 7-8: Building Momentum

- [ ] First project completed; artifact created and shared (PR merged / doc published / milestone delivered).
- [ ] Applied feedback from manager (visible behavior change or artifact).
- [ ] Applied feedback from peer or cross-functional stakeholder.
- [ ] Second project initiated or responsibility scope increased.
- [ ] Day-60 check-in scheduled.

### Day-60 Success Criteria (Gate to Execute Phase)

- [ ] Completed first project successfully — artifact exists.
- [ ] Received positive feedback from at least one team member (structured or unsolicited).
- [ ] Works with minimal supervision on familiar tasks within role scope.
- [ ] Demonstrates core job competencies at the expected level for role/level.
- [ ] Goals for days 61-90 documented and agreed with manager.

---

## Days 61-90: Execute Phase

### Weeks 9-10: Independence

- [ ] Handling most responsibilities without requiring guidance for familiar tasks.
- [ ] Major project or milestone in progress (defined at day-60 review).
- [ ] Proactively identified and communicated at least one opportunity or risk.
- [ ] Contributing to team planning activities.
- [ ] Day-45 skip-level completed (if not already scheduled — schedule now).

### Weeks 11-12: Full Performance

- [ ] Major project or milestone delivered — artifact exists.
- [ ] Working autonomously on 80%+ of responsibilities.
- [ ] Development plan for next quarter drafted and reviewed with manager.
- [ ] 90-day review scheduled.
- [ ] Onboarding satisfaction survey sent to hire.

### Day-90 Success Criteria (Formal Review)

- [ ] Meets or exceeds performance expectations on primary responsibilities.
- [ ] Works autonomously 80%+ of time without guidance.
- [ ] Major project delivered with verifiable artifact.
- [ ] Development plan for Q+1 agreed with manager.
- [ ] Integrated into team culture — participates in social and professional rituals.

**Day-90 Review Outcome:** [ ] Standard performance cycle begins | [ ] Extended ramp — formal PIP or extended review:
