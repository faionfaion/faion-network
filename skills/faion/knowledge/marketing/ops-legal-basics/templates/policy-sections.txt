TERMS OF SERVICE — REQUIRED SECTIONS

1. Acceptance of Terms
   - Using the service constitutes agreement to these terms

2. Description of Service
   - What you provide and what you do not guarantee

3. User Responsibilities
   - Acceptable use policy
   - Account security and credentials

4. Payment Terms
   - Pricing, billing cycle, cancellation policy
   - Automatic renewal notice (specify days in advance)

5. Intellectual Property
   - Who owns what (customer data vs. platform IP)
   - License grant from user to platform (for UGC/SaaS)

6. Disclaimers
   - No warranty of any kind (express or implied)
   - Limitation of liability (cap at subscription fees paid)

7. Termination
   - How either party can terminate
   - Data export / deletion upon termination

8. Governing Law
   - Jurisdiction and applicable law
   - Dispute resolution method (arbitration, court, jurisdiction)

9. Severability + Entire Agreement
   - One unenforceable clause does not void the whole agreement
   - Written terms supersede all verbal side-agreements

PRIVACY POLICY — REQUIRED SECTIONS

1. Information Collected
   - What personal data is collected
   - How it is collected (forms, cookies, analytics, third-party SDKs)

2. Use of Information
   - Purpose of each data category
   - Legal basis for processing (GDPR: consent / contract / legitimate interest)

3. Data Sharing
   - Named third parties (processors, analytics vendors, payment processors)
   - Purpose and legal basis for each transfer

4. Data Retention
   - How long each category is retained and why

5. User Rights
   - Access, correction, deletion, portability (GDPR Art. 15–20)
   - Opt-out of sale (CCPA)
   - How to submit requests and response timeline

6. Security
   - Technical and organizational measures description (without specifics that aid attackers)

7. Cookies
   - Cookie types (essential, analytics, marketing)
   - Consent mechanism (link to Cookie Policy or inline)

8. Children
   - Age restriction statement (COPPA compliance if applicable)

9. Contact
   - Controller identity (legal name + address)
   - DPO contact if required (GDPR Art. 37)
   - Effective Date and Version number at top of document
