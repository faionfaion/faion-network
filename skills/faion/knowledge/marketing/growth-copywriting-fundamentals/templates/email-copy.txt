# Email Copy Structure

Subject: [Curiosity or benefit hook — test 5 variants, pick the most specific]
Preview text: [Adds information not in subject; do not repeat the subject line]

Hey [Name],

[Opening line — connects to their world, a pain point, or a recent event. Not a greeting.]

[1-2 sentences: the problem or observation you're addressing]

[Transition to solution or story — 1 sentence]

[Key benefit 1 — active voice, outcome-first]
[Key benefit 2 — active voice, outcome-first]
[Key benefit 3 — active voice, outcome-first]

[CTA — one clear action, specific and benefit-oriented]
[LINK]

[Sign off]

P.S. [Create urgency, add a secondary benefit, or repeat the key offer]

---
# Word count targets:
# Promotional/launch email: 150-300 words
# Nurture/value email: 200-400 words
# Re-engagement email: 75-150 words (shorter = more personal)
#
# Rules:
# - Write subject line LAST (after body is final)
# - Send from personal name, not brand name
# - One CTA only — do not add "also check out..."
# - P.S. line gets high read rate — treat as second CTA
