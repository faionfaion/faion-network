# Meta Naming Convention

Format: `[stage]_[product]_[audience]_[creative]_[yyyymmdd]`

- stage: tofu | mofu | bofu | retention
- product: short-slug, lowercase, dash-only
- audience: short-slug describing the segment
- creative: short-slug describing the asset
- yyyymmdd: launch date

Example: `bofu_pro_cart_abandoners_video_20260523`
