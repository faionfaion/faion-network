# Activation Email Sequence

Send to users who have not yet activated. One CTA per email.

## Email 1: Welcome (Day 0 — on signup)

**Subject:** Welcome to [Product]! Here's your first step
**Goal:** Drive to the single most important first action

Content:
- Personalized greeting
- Single CTA: "Create your first [X]" — links directly to the action
- Expected time: "Takes 2 minutes"

## Email 2: Quick Win (Day 1 — if not activated)

**Subject:** Quick win: Do this in 5 minutes
**Goal:** Show one high-value use case with steps

Content:
- Focus on one use case matching signup intent
- Step-by-step with screenshots
- Social proof: "10,000 users did this today"

## Email 3: Help Offer (Day 3 — if not activated)

**Subject:** Need help getting started?
**Goal:** Remove blockers with support options

Content:
- Short video tutorial (90 seconds)
- Option to book onboarding call
- FAQ links for top 3 questions

## Email 4: Last Push (Day 7 — if not activated)

**Subject:** Your [Product] account is waiting
**Goal:** Urgency + social proof

Content:
- Recap the core value proposition in 2 sentences
- Success story from a user with similar profile
- Direct support CTA: "Reply to this email"
