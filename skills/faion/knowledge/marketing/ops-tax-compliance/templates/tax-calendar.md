# US Tax Calendar — Solopreneur / LLC Reference

## Federal Deadlines

| Date | Action | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| January 15 | Q4 estimated tax payment | Prior year Q4 (Sep–Dec) |
| January 31 | 1099-NEC due to contractors | Also W-2s if you have employees |
| March 15 | S-Corp / Partnership return (Form 1120-S / 1065) | Or extension Form 7004 |
| April 15 | Individual / LLC return (Form 1040 + Sch C) | Also Q1 estimated payment |
| April 15 | FBAR due (FinCEN 114) | If foreign financial accounts > $10K |
| April 15 | HSA contribution deadline | For prior year |
| June 15 | Q2 estimated tax payment | Covers Apr–May income |
| September 15 | Q3 estimated tax payment | Covers Jun–Aug income |
| September 15 | S-Corp / Partnership extended return due | If extension was filed March 15 |
| October 15 | Individual extended return due | If extension was filed April 15 |
| December 31 | Solo 401(k) contribution deadline | Employee deferral portion |
| January 15 (next year) | Q4 estimated tax payment | Covers Sep–Dec income |

## Year-End Action Deadlines

| Action | Deadline | Impact |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Retirement contributions (Solo 401(k) employee) | December 31 | Reduces current-year taxable income |
| Retirement contributions (SEP-IRA) | April 15 (or extension) | Reduces prior-year taxable income |
| Section 179 equipment purchases | December 31 | Full deduction in current year |
| Accelerate deductible expenses | December 31 | Pay before year-end to deduct this year |
| Defer income | December 31 | Invoice late December → collect January |
| S-Corp election (Form 2553) | March 15 for current year | Must file by 2 months 15 days after tax year start |

## Quarterly Payment Schedule (Quick Reference)

```
Q1 (Jan–Mar income) → Pay by April 15
Q2 (Apr–May income) → Pay by June 15
Q3 (Jun–Aug income) → Pay by September 15
Q4 (Sep–Dec income) → Pay by January 15 of next year
```

Note: April 15 covers BOTH the prior-year tax return AND the Q1 estimated payment.
These are separate obligations with separate forms (1040 for return, 1040-ES for estimate).

## Penalty Quick Reference

| Penalty | Rate | Trigger |
|---------|------|---------|
| Underpayment (est. tax) | Federal short-term rate + 3% | Owe > $1K at filing without safe-harbor coverage |
| Late filing | 5% per month (max 25%) | Return not filed by deadline |
| Late payment | 0.5% per month | Tax not paid by deadline |
| Accuracy | 20% of underpayment | Substantial understatement or negligence |
