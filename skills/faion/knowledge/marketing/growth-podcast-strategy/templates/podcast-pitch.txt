- [Specific point 1 — concrete, not generic]
- [Specific point 2 — concrete, not generic]
- [Specific point 3 — concrete, not generic]

I've appeared on [podcast name] and [podcast name] — here's a clip: [link].

Would this be a fit for [Podcast Name]?

[Your name]

---
# Rules:
# - Under 200 words total
# - No "I'm a huge fan" opener
# - Reference one specific episode by name (not just the show)
# - Lead with audience value, not your credentials
# - Include a previous appearance or media page link
# - Prepare 3 distinct topic angles; lead with the strongest fit for this show
