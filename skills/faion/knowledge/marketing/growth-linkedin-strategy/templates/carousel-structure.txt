# LinkedIn Carousel Structure (10 Slides)

Slide 1: HOOK
[Bold statement or provocative question]
[Subtext: what the reader will learn]

Slide 2: PROMISE
"In this guide, you'll learn..."
[3 bullet points: what they'll get]

Slides 3-8: VALUE (one point per slide)
[Point title]
[2-3 sentences of explanation or example]
[Optional: one visual, diagram, or short list]

Slide 9: SUMMARY
"To recap..."
[3-5 bullet points of the key takeaways]

Slide 10: CTA
"Follow [your name] for more [topic]"
"Save this post for later"
"Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you [resource]"

---

# Notes
- Carousels have the highest save rate on LinkedIn — prioritize evergreen frameworks
- First slide text must work as a standalone post hook (it appears in feed)
- Each slide should be readable in 3 seconds — one idea per slide maximum
- Design in Canva or Figma; upload as a PDF (LinkedIn renders it as swipeable)
