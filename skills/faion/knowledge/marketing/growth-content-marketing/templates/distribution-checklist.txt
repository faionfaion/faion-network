# Content Distribution Checklist

## Day 1 (Publish)
- [ ] Publish on blog with all SEO metadata (title, meta description, canonical)
- [ ] Send to email list (subject line tested in preview)
- [ ] Share on Twitter/X with custom hook (not just the title)
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn with a different hook than Twitter
- [ ] Post in 1-2 relevant communities (Reddit, Slack, Discord) — value-first framing

## Week 1
- [ ] Repurpose into Twitter/X thread (5-7 tweets from key points)
- [ ] Reach out to anyone mentioned or quoted in the post
- [ ] Submit to 2-3 relevant industry newsletters for inclusion
- [ ] Answer related questions on Reddit/Quora with link to post
- [ ] Add internal links from 3+ existing high-traffic posts to this one

## Ongoing (monthly review)
- [ ] Add post to topical content hub page
- [ ] Monitor keyword ranking (Search Console)
- [ ] Update with new data or examples if ranking position drops
- [ ] Build backlinks via guest post or journalist outreach if post has traction
