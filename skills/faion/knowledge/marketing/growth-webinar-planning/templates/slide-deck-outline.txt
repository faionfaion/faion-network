# Webinar Slide Deck Outline (60-minute format)

Slide 1: Title + Date + Host name
Slide 2: About You (brief — 2-3 credentials, not full bio)
Slide 3: Agenda — What We'll Cover Today (3-5 bullets)
Slide 4: Poll — Engagement question relevant to topic
Slide 5: The Problem — Hook statement, make them feel the pain
Slides 6-8: Section 1 — [First key point, 3 sub-points max per slide]
Slides 9-11: Section 2 — [Second key point, 3 sub-points max per slide]
Slides 12-14: Section 3 — [Third key point, 3 sub-points max per slide]
Slide 15: Recap — 3 key takeaways in bullet form
Slide 16: Your Solution — Soft introduction to product/service
Slide 17: Offer Details — What you're offering, price, bonuses
Slide 18: How to Get Started — 3-step process, CTA
Slide 19: Q&A — "What questions do you have?"
Slide 20: Thank You + Replay/CTA + Where to find you
