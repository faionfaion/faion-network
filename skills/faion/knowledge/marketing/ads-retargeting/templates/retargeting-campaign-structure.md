# Retargeting Campaign Structure: [Product]

## Campaign: Retargeting — Full Funnel

Exclude: Purchasers (90d) from ALL ad sets below.

---

### Ad Set 1: Visitors — Cold (30-180 days)

- Audience: All visitors 180d, exclude product/pricing page viewers
- Exclude: Purchasers 90d, active trial users
- Message: Brand reminder + value proposition
- Budget: $[X]/day
- Frequency cap: 3/week
- Creative: [Brand awareness creative name]

---

### Ad Set 2: Engaged — Warm (7-30 days)

- Audience: Product page viewers 30d + pricing page viewers 14d
- Exclude: Cart abandoners 7d, purchasers 90d
- Message: Benefits, demo offer, social proof
- Budget: $[X]/day
- Frequency cap: 5/week
- Creative: [Benefits/demo creative name]

---

### Ad Set 3: Hot — Very High Intent (1-7 days)

- Audience: Cart abandoners 7d, checkout starters 7d, lead form openers 14d
- Exclude: Purchasers 30d
- Message: Urgency, discount or free shipping, remove friction
- Budget: $[X]/day
- Frequency cap: 7/week for first week, then 5/week
- Creative: [Urgency creative name]
