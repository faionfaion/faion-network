# research-pipeline-prompt.txt — XML prompt for multi-stage market research pipeline

<research_pipeline>
  <topic>{{research_topic}}</topic>
  <stages>
    <stage name="exploration" tool="perplexity">{{exploration_question}}</stage>
    <stage name="competitor-intel" tool="serpapi">{{competitor_question}}</stage>
    <stage name="trend" tool="google-trends">{{trend_question}}</stage>
    <stage name="synthesis" tool="claude-sonnet">Merge stage outputs into a structured report with source registry</stage>
  </stages>
  <output>Markdown report, source registry, coverage gaps list</output>
</research_pipeline>

## Variables
- {{research_topic}} — the market or product area being researched
- {{exploration_question}} — broad landscape question for Perplexity
- {{competitor_question}} — specific competitor intel question for SerpAPI
- {{trend_question}} — trend search terms and time window for Google Trends

## Notes
- Each stage must declare its tool explicitly; no inferred assignments
- Synthesis is always a separate stage with Claude, never embedded in tool calls
- Source registry must list tool, URL, and access date for every claim
- Coverage gaps list must be explicit: "no data found" per stage, not omitted
