# market-sizing-prompt.txt — XML prompt for AI-assisted market sizing tasks

<market_sizing_task>
  <market>{{market_definition}}</market>
  <approach>Run top-down and bottom-up independently. Do not share intermediate results between paths.</approach>
  <top_down>{{top_down_instruction}}</top_down>
  <bottom_up>{{bottom_up_instruction}}</bottom_up>
  <output>
    - TAM, SAM, SOM with confidence range (low/mid/high)
    - Assumption log (one line per assumption)
    - Source list with access date
    - Flag any source older than 12 months
  </output>
</market_sizing_task>

## Variables
- {{market_definition}} — precise market scope (geography, customer type, segment, year)
- {{top_down_instruction}} — e.g. "Find total knowledge-worker software market → isolate AI tools segment → apply addressable fraction"
- {{bottom_up_instruction}} — e.g. "Estimate target companies × seats × annual price"

## Notes
- Define TAM, SAM, SOM explicitly in the prompt on every run
- Require confidence ranges (low/mid/high), not point estimates
- A human must review the assumption log before the estimate enters any deliverable
