# CoT trigger phrases — append to user message or system prompt

# Zero-shot (cheapest, try first):
Think step by step. Show your reasoning before giving the final answer.

# Tree of Thoughts (branching decisions):
Generate 3 distinct approaches to this problem, evaluate each on relevance
and soundness (score 1-10), then choose the best approach and complete the
solution.

# Least-to-Most (sequential sub-dependencies):
First list the subproblems from simplest to most complex. Then solve each
in order, using previous solutions as context for later ones.

# Self-Consistency trigger (add to sampling loop):
Think step by step and give your final answer. (sample N times at temp=0.7,
then take majority vote over final answers)
