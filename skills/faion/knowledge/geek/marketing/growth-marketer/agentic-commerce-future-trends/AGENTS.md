# Agentic Commerce and Future Trends

## Summary

AI agents autonomously complete entire purchase flows — comparing options, making recommendations, executing transactions — without human search. Businesses must optimize for trust signals and task completion (not just keywords) to be selected by AI agents. Traditional SEO optimizes for keywords; agentic commerce optimizes for genuine authority, cross-platform consistency, and machine-readable interfaces.

## Why

25% of organic traffic is projected to shift to AI assistants by end of 2026. AI agents evaluate businesses on earned media, review quality, entity authority, cross-platform consistency, and API availability — factors that are harder to game than keywords and backlinks. Businesses optimizing only for traditional SEO will be invisible to AI-mediated purchase flows. The transition is gradual but irreversible.

## When To Use

- Auditing a business's readiness for AI-agent-mediated discovery and purchase.
- Building an earned media and review strategy that signals authority to AI systems.
- Designing API-first or machine-readable interfaces for AI agent access.
- Planning multi-format content strategy (text, video, audio, structured data) for AI source diversity.

## When NOT To Use

- Pure keyword SEO campaigns with no budget for earned media or review collection — not the right tool for short-term rank chasing.
- Very early-stage businesses with no existing authority signals — build fundamentals first.
- B2C impulse-purchase categories where agentic commerce adoption is still minimal.
