# Free / Marketing Fundamentals Playbooks

First-customers and early positioning playbooks. Audience: first-time founders, side-hustlers, marketers turning builder. Citation scope: `knowledge/free/` only.

## Playbooks

| Slug | Goal |
|------|------|
| `first-10-customers` | Hand-source 10 paying users without ads using warm intros, cold DMs, and manual onboarding |
| `landing-page-essentials` | Write and ship a one-page site that converts visitors into signups |
| `positioning-basics` | Write a one-sentence positioning statement that makes your offer obvious to one target persona |
