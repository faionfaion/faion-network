# Free / MVP Essentials Playbooks

Ship the smallest thing. Audience: first-time founders, returning developers, side-hustlers cutting scope before their first launch. Citation scope: `knowledge/free/` only.

## Playbooks

| Slug | Goal |
|------|------|
| `mvp-scope-cutting` | Reduce a 30-item feature list to ≤5 shippable items |
| `ugly-first-version` | Build the ugliest viable implementation of your 5 M items |
| `mvp-launch-checklist` | Gate-by-gate checklist before calling your MVP live |
