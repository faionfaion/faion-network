# Hold a focused 1:1 with a team member

## Context

A 30-min recurring 1:1 that produces (a) one growth or blocker insight, (b) one decision or commitment from the PM, (c) updated team-development notes. Not a status update — explicitly not.

Tier: **solo**. Complexity: **light**. Group: **role-project-manager**. Persona: **role-project-manager**.

## Outcome

This playbook is done when:

- First 5 minutes have zero status content.
- One growth or blocker topic owned by them.
- One PM commitment written down.
- Per-person growth tracker updated within 24h.

## Steps

### 1. Refuse status mode

Set the conversation tone before the meeting starts.

Tasks:
- Open with a non-work question.
- Ban status updates explicitly ('we have the standup for that').
- Frame the time as theirs to spend.

Outputs:
- Tone-setting opener.

Decision gate: Advance when the first 5 minutes have zero status content.

### 2. Pick one growth thread

One growth or blocker topic, not five.

Tasks:
- Surface the topic that matters most to them this week.
- Use a question-bank cue if needed (tenure-appropriate).
- Probe with 'what would make that easier?'.

Outputs:
- Growth/blocker topic.

Decision gate: Advance when the topic is named and they own framing it.

### 3. Decide one thing

PM exits with one decision or commitment.

Tasks:
- Convert their need into one PM action.
- Make the commitment explicit ('I will do X by date').
- Write it down on the spot.

Outputs:
- PM commitment.

Decision gate: Advance when the commitment is written down in their view.

### 4. Risk + signal note

Capture anything risk-relevant.

Tasks:
- Note any retention or burnout signal.
- Note any project risk surfaced.
- File to the appropriate register.

Outputs:
- Risk/signal note.

Decision gate: Advance when notes are filed in the right register (not lost in the 1:1 doc).

### 5. Update team-development log

Track patterns across 1:1s.

Tasks:
- Update the per-person growth tracker.
- Note recurring themes for quarterly review.
- Schedule any cross-1:1 follow-up.

Outputs:
- Team-development log entry.

Decision gate: Required: per-person growth tracker updated within 24 hours.

## Decision points

- Sync vs async fallback — sync default; async fallback only when timezone or remote constraints make sync useless.
- Coach vs decide — coach first; decide only when they ask you to.
