## Scope

Move from generic 'full-stack dev' to a paid specialist position (e.g. headless-commerce on Shopify Hydrogen, or AI integrations for SMB SaaS) with 2 case studies, a tightened portfolio, and a 30-50% higher day rate.

## Stages

### 1. Pick the niche

Use the niche scorecard, not a hunch.

Tasks:
- List candidate niches (e.g. headless commerce, AI integrations)
- Score each on demand + supply + your fit
- Pick one

Outputs:
- Candidate list
- Score sheet
- Chosen niche

Decision gate: Advance only with one niche named and one scorecard filled.

### 2. Buyer interviews

10 calls with people who pay specialists.

Tasks:
- Find 10 buyers in the niche
- Run buyer-interview scripts
- Capture buying triggers, vendors, budgets

Outputs:
- 10 buyer interviews
- Triggers + vendors log
- Budget data

Decision gate: Advance only when buying signal is concrete.

### 3. Skill upgrade plan

Close the gap to a credible specialist.

Tasks:
- Map required skills against current ones
- Pick 2-3 gaps to close in 8 weeks
- Build with practice projects

Outputs:
- Skill gap map
- Learning plan
- Practice projects

Decision gate: Advance only when practice projects ship publicly.

### 4. Case study harvest

Convert old work into niche-relevant case studies.

Tasks:
- Pick 2 past projects relevant to the niche
- Re-write them as buyer-focused case studies
- Add case-study clause to future contracts

Outputs:
- 2 case studies
- Updated portfolio
- Case-study clause template

Decision gate: Advance only when 2 case studies are publishable.

### 5. Positioning overhaul

Re-write everything around the niche.

Tasks:
- Re-write LinkedIn, Upwork, website
- Tighten the one-liner
- Publish a niche manifesto post

Outputs:
- Updated profiles
- New one-liner
- Manifesto post

Decision gate: Advance only when 3 outsiders can guess the niche from your profile.

### 6. Content + topical authority

Publish in the niche, weekly.

Tasks:
- Pick 6-8 anchor topics
- Publish weekly across X + LinkedIn + blog
- Track keyword rankings + inbound DMs

Outputs:
- Topic plan
- Weekly publish log
- Keyword + DM metrics

Decision gate: Advance only when weekly cadence runs 6+ weeks.

### 7. Partnership + community

Get inside the niches gravity wells.

Tasks:
- Identify 2-3 communities + 1-2 strategic partners
- Show up consistently + offer value
- Pitch a podcast or guest post in the niche

Outputs:
- Community presence log
- Partner intros
- Podcast/guest post

Decision gate: Advance only when at least one partner or podcast booking lands.

### 8. Rate jump + first specialist clients

Land work at the new rate.

Tasks:
- Quote new clients at the higher rate
- Hold firm on price during negotiation
- Close 1-2 specialist engagements

Outputs:
- New rate cards
- Negotiation notes
- 1-2 specialist contracts

Decision gate: Advance only when at least one client books at the new rate.

### 9. Lock the pivot

Decline non-niche work and audit traction.

Tasks:
- Define explicit decline criteria for non-niche work
- Run a quarterly traction audit
- Commit to year-2 plan in the niche

Outputs:
- Decline criteria
- Traction audit
- Year-2 plan

Decision gate: Cycle closes when pivot is committed and non-niche work is being declined.

## Common pitfalls

- Skipping the decision-gate write-up to keep moving - closes the loop with vibes, not evidence.
- Treating each stages outputs as optional - every output is a gate input for the next stage.
- Letting one bad week stretch a fixed-cadence ritual into a quarterly one.

## Quality checklist

- Every stage has at least one referenced methodology that resolves under `knowledge/`.
- Every output is a real artefact, not a feeling.
- The final decision is a written commitment, not we will see.
