# Freelance-to-SaaS transition (6-month side build)

## Context

This playbook covers the global flow for the persona `p3-technical-freelancer`. It applies whenever the trigger described in the scope below appears in the operator's workflow. The output is a written, auditable artefact pack, not a verbal hand-wave.

## Outcome

Across two quarters, parallel to delivery work: pick one client-pain pattern, validate it as a productized SaaS, ship a paid v1 to 5-10 customers, and decide whether to scale, sunset, or fold into the freelance offer.
