# Reputation + referral pipeline ramp (90-day flywheel)

## Context

This playbook covers the global flow for the persona `p3-technical-freelancer`. It applies whenever the trigger described in the scope below appears in the operator's workflow. The output is a written, auditable artefact pack, not a verbal hand-wave.

## Outcome

Build a system that compounds: every closed engagement produces a testimonial + a referral + a public artefact, so by day 90 inbound covers >=50% of new pipeline and outbound effort drops.

## Steps

Define the scope, exit criteria, and the people who must agree on success.

- Restate the scope outcome for this engagement in one sentence.
- Identify who owns the scope output and who must approve it.
- Produce the scope artefact in the agreed format.
