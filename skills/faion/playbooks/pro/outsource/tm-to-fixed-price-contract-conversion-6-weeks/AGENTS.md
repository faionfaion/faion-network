# T&M to fixed-price contract conversion (6 weeks)

## Context

This playbook covers the global flow for the persona `p4-outsource-specialist`. It applies whenever the trigger described in the scope below appears in the operator's workflow. The output is a written, auditable artefact pack, not a verbal hand-wave.

## Outcome

Engagement is running on time-and-materials. Client requests conversion to fixed-price for the next phase. By end of week 6 Daria has: requirements baseline frozen for phase 2, three-point estimate with risk reserves, defensible commercial proposal, and a signed contract amendment without the team taking a margin haircut.
