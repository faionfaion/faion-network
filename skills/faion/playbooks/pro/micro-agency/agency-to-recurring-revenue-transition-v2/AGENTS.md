# Agency-to-recurring-revenue transition (6 months)

## Context

Move the agency from majority project-based revenue to majority retainer/productized recurring revenue. Outcome: at least 50% MRR-style recurring by month 6, founder time spent on delivery cut by 40%, gross margin up by 10pp.

## Outcome

By the end of this playbook, the operator has run the 6 stages below and produced the written decision artefact in the final stage.

Success criteria:

- All 6 stages have written outputs in the project record
- Each stage's decision gate was answered before advancing (yes / no in writing)
- Final stage produced the required written decision artifact
- Every methodology reference loaded cleanly via `faion get-content`

## Steps

### 1. Audit Today's Mix

Quantify the starting point.

Tasks:
- Compute revenue split: project vs retainer vs productized
- Compute margin and founder-hours per type
- Set a 6-month target: ≥50% MRR-style by month 6

Outputs:
- revenue mix table
- margin + hours table
- 6-month target memo

Decision gate: Advance only when target is documented and approved by the founder.

### 2. Pick 2 Recurring Products

Decide what you'll sell on retainer.

Tasks:
- List candidate retainer / productized offerings
- Pick 2 with the strongest repeat-buying signal
- Define scope, price, and gross-margin floor per offering

Outputs:
- candidate list
- chosen 2
- scope+price+margin per offering

Decision gate: Advance when 2 offerings have a written scope/price/margin model.

### 3. Migrate Existing Book

Convert current project clients to recurring where possible.

Tasks:
- Identify 5-8 project clients that fit the new retainer offers
- Pitch the retainer to each; sign at least 3
- Wind down the project-only engagements that don't fit

Outputs:
- migration list
- 3 signed retainers
- wind-down plan

Decision gate: Advance when ≥3 retainers are signed and start dates are on calendar.

### 4. Acquire New Recurring Customers

Build the inbound and outbound motion.

Tasks:
- Ship landing pages for each recurring offering with conversion tracking
- Run a 30-day outbound campaign to 50 ICP prospects
- Convert at least 2 net-new logos onto retainer

Outputs:
- live landing pages
- outbound log
- 2 new retainer logos

Decision gate: Advance only when the recurring funnel is producing ≥1 qualified lead/week.

### 5. Operational Restructure

Stop running the agency like a project shop.

Tasks:
- Cut founder delivery hours by 40% via SOPs and contractor handoffs
- Rebuild forecast as MRR + project + productized lines
- Set the weekly recurring-revenue dashboard

Outputs:
- SOPs in place
- MRR-first forecast
- weekly dashboard

Decision gate: Advance when founder delivery hours have measurably dropped vs month 1.

### 6. 6-Month Review

Did the transition actually happen?

Tasks:
- Recompute revenue mix; confirm ≥50% MRR-style
- Compute gross-margin delta and founder-hour delta
- Decide: double down / hold / unwind

Outputs:
- final mix table
- delta computation
- double-down/hold/unwind memo

Decision gate: Required output: written go-forward decision with evidence.

## Decision points

- Stage 1 (Audit Today's Mix): Advance only when target is documented and approved by the founder.
- Stage 2 (Pick 2 Recurring Products): Advance when 2 offerings have a written scope/price/margin model.
- Stage 3 (Migrate Existing Book): Advance when ≥3 retainers are signed and start dates are on calendar.
- Stage 4 (Acquire New Recurring Customers): Advance only when the recurring funnel is producing ≥1 qualified lead/week.
- Stage 5 (Operational Restructure): Advance when founder delivery hours have measurably dropped vs month 1.
- Stage 6 (6-Month Review): Required output: written go-forward decision with evidence.
