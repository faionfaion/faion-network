# Productize a service offering (8–10 weeks idea to first paid sale)

## Context

Take a recurring custom engagement and convert it into a named, fixed-scope, fixed-price productized service with a public landing page, sales script, delivery SOP, and at least one paying customer.

## Outcome

By the end of this playbook, the operator has run the 6 stages below and produced the written decision artefact in the final stage.

Success criteria:

- All 6 stages have written outputs in the project record
- Each stage's decision gate was answered before advancing (yes / no in writing)
- Final stage produced the required written decision artifact
- Every methodology reference loaded cleanly via `faion get-content`

## Steps

### 1. Discover

Pick the recurring engagement to productize.

Tasks:
- Audit last 6 months of project work and tag repeated scope patterns
- Pick one bundle that you've delivered ≥3 times with positive margin
- Interview 5 past clients on the painful before-state

Outputs:
- Repeat-work audit table
- Chosen productization candidate
- 5 client-pain notes

Decision gate: Advance only if one candidate scored ≥3 repeats AND ≥3 client confirmations.

### 2. Scope & Price

Lock fixed scope and fixed price.

Tasks:
- Draft the productized-service canvas (problem, deliverables, exclusions, timeline)
- Run a cost build-up; set price at 3-4x your direct delivery cost
- Define 3 explicit non-goals to protect scope

Outputs:
- service canvas
- pricing memo
- non-goals list

Decision gate: Advance if margin model clears target gross margin and scope fits on one page.

### 3. Landing & Sales Script

Public landing + repeatable sales motion.

Tasks:
- Build single-page landing with outcome-led copy and one CTA
- Write a sales-call script (problem, deliverables, price, objections, close)
- Set conversion tracking on the CTA

Outputs:
- live landing URL
- sales-call script
- tracking dashboard

Decision gate: Advance when landing converts at least one qualified lead per 50 visits.

### 4. Delivery SOP

Make delivery repeatable, not artisanal.

Tasks:
- Document the delivery workflow as a numbered SOP with checkpoints
- Define handover artifacts at each checkpoint
- Write the case-study template the SOP outputs by default

Outputs:
- delivery SOP
- checkpoint artifacts
- case-study template

Decision gate: Advance when a contractor can follow the SOP unaided to checkpoint 1.

### 5. First Paid Sale

Convert one prospect end-to-end at the fixed price.

Tasks:
- Run outbound to 20 warm prospects with the new landing
- Hold sales calls using the script; do not discount the fixed price
- Sign one fixed-price contract and kick off delivery

Outputs:
- 20 outbound logs
- 1 signed contract
- kickoff packet

Decision gate: Advance when 1 client has paid the first invoice.

### 6. Learn & Iterate

Capture what the first sale taught.

Tasks:
- Compare delivered scope vs canvas — log every overage
- Update SOP, landing copy, and price for the next cycle
- Decide: scale this productized offering, or kill and pick another

Outputs:
- overage log
- v2 canvas + landing diff
- scale/kill memo

Decision gate: Required output: a written scale-or-kill decision with evidence.

## Decision points

- Stage 1 (Discover): Advance only if one candidate scored ≥3 repeats AND ≥3 client confirmations.
- Stage 2 (Scope & Price): Advance if margin model clears target gross margin and scope fits on one page.
- Stage 3 (Landing & Sales Script): Advance when landing converts at least one qualified lead per 50 visits.
- Stage 4 (Delivery SOP): Advance when a contractor can follow the SOP unaided to checkpoint 1.
- Stage 5 (First Paid Sale): Advance when 1 client has paid the first invoice.
- Stage 6 (Learn & Iterate): Required output: a written scale-or-kill decision with evidence.
