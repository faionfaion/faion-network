## Jira AI (Atlassian Intelligence)

**Key Features:**
- **Rovo Agents** - AI-powered teammates that learn how teams work
- **AI Work Breakdown** - Recommend subtasks and child issues
- **AI Work Creation** - Create issues from Confluence pages or chat
- **Workflow Builder** - Auto-create transitions, statuses, rules
- **Generative AI Editor** - Draft content, adjust tone, fix grammar
- **Virtual Service Agent** - Slack, Teams, email, help center integration

**2026 Developments:**
- Rovo available to all customers
- Rewatch acquisition -> meeting recording, AI notes
- Secoda acquisition -> data enrichment
- DX acquisition -> engineering intelligence
