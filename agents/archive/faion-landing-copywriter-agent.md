## Copy Rules

1. **Benefits > Features**
   - Feature: "256-bit encryption"
   - Benefit: "Your data stays private, always"

2. **Specificity sells**
   - Weak: "Save money"
   - Strong: "Save $2,847/year on average"

3. **One idea per sentence**
   - Short sentences. Easy to scan. Quick to understand.

4. **Power words**
   - Free, New, Proven, Guaranteed, Instant, Easy, Secret, Discover

5. **Address objections**
   - Price â†’ value comparison, ROI, guarantee
   - Time â†’ quick setup, done-for-you
   - Trust â†’ testimonials, logos, security badges
   - Complexity â†’ simple steps, support included
